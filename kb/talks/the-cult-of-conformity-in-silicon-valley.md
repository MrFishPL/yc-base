# The Cult of Conformity in Silicon Valley — Compiled Truth

Michael Seibel and Dalton Caldwell argue tech has shifted from a home for non-conformist weirdos to a mainstream, status-seeking career track, and discuss how to spot and cultivate genuine non-conformist founders.
