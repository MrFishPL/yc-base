# Zepto: How Two 17-Year-Olds Built India's Largest Seller Of Fruits and Vegetables — Compiled Truth

Zepto co-founder Aadit Palicha explains how a COVID-era WhatsApp grocery delivery group in Mumbai turned into a 10-minute delivery giant through relentless doorstep customer conversations, a pivot to owning dark stores, and now heavy AI use across supply chain and operations.
