# Critiquing Software Startup Websites with CEO of Glide — Compiled Truth

David from Glide and a YC host critique several developer-tool startup landing pages live, extracting principles for how dev-tool companies should present product, proof, and calls to action.

**Key ideas (with timestamps):**
- For developer tools, social proof like GitHub stars and repo velocity matters more than typical marketing trust signals [critiquing-software-startup-websites-with-ceo-of-glide @ 00:42](https://youtu.be/CYdsh1FhpI4?t=42)
- Code samples on a dev-tool site should be syntax-highlighted and at least as legible as the marketing copy, not shrunk down as an afterthought [critiquing-software-startup-websites-with-ceo-of-glide @ 02:11](https://youtu.be/CYdsh1FhpI4?t=131)
- Having pricing on a site is "the difference between a project and a product" and signals a company you can safely put into production [critiquing-software-startup-websites-with-ceo-of-glide @ 06:47](https://youtu.be/CYdsh1FhpI4?t=407)
- More than six active GitHub contributors, not just the founder, is a real health signal of open-source traction [critiquing-software-startup-websites-with-ceo-of-glide @ 09:09](https://youtu.be/CYdsh1FhpI4?t=549)
- It's almost always a mistake for a product site not to show an actual screenshot of the product on the homepage [critiquing-software-startup-websites-with-ceo-of-glide @ 11:34](https://youtu.be/CYdsh1FhpI4?t=694)
- Early-stage dev-tool sites should show the product and code examples first, since visitors are developers; later-stage sites can lean on testimonials and "book a demo" CTAs aimed at executives [critiquing-software-startup-websites-with-ceo-of-glide @ 12:13](https://youtu.be/CYdsh1FhpI4?t=733)
- Sites should lead with the single most exciting, differentiated claim immediately instead of burying it under vague copy [critiquing-software-startup-websites-with-ceo-of-glide @ 15:37](https://youtu.be/CYdsh1FhpI4?t=937)
- Founders should be hyper-available to early customers (chat widget, personal cell number) since responsiveness is a small company's real advantage over incumbents [critiquing-software-startup-websites-with-ceo-of-glide @ 17:13](https://youtu.be/CYdsh1FhpI4?t=1033)
- Alternating background colors and clear margins between page sections prevent screenshots and interactive demos from visually bleeding into surrounding content [critiquing-software-startup-websites-with-ceo-of-glide @ 20:36](https://youtu.be/CYdsh1FhpI4?t=1236)
- Developer audiences are technical and skeptical, so dev-tool sites should lead with concrete technical details rather than vague benefit statements [critiquing-software-startup-websites-with-ceo-of-glide @ 22:16](https://youtu.be/CYdsh1FhpI4?t=1336)
