# Anthropic Co-founder: Building Claude Code, Lessons From GPT-3 & LLM System Design — Compiled Truth

Anthropic co-founder Tom Brown recounts his path from early startup employee to OpenAI GPT-3 scaling engineer to Anthropic co-founder, and explains how Claude Code and Anthropic's coding strength emerged from treating the model itself as a user.

**Key ideas (with timestamps):**
- Working at an early-stage startup without anyone telling you what to do teaches a "wolves not dogs" mindset of having to hunt for your own success, which Brown calls the most valuable mindset shift for doing bigger things [anthropic-co-founder-building-claude-code-lessons-from-gpt-3 @ 01:58](https://youtu.be/JdT78t1Offo?t=118)
- Brown convinced himself to leave startups for AI research after seeing scaling laws show a straight line of more intelligence from more compute over 12 orders of magnitude, which pivoted all his work into scaling [anthropic-co-founder-building-claude-code-lessons-from-gpt-3 @ 14:12](https://youtu.be/JdT78t1Offo?t=852)
- Anthropic's seven co-founders left OpenAI's safety and scaling orgs believing there would be a critical handoff of control to transformative AI and wanting to build an institution that could handle those stakes responsibly [anthropic-co-founder-building-claude-code-lessons-from-gpt-3 @ 16:22](https://youtu.be/JdT78t1Offo?t=982)
- Anthropic's culture has scaled to 2,000 people without much internal politics because the first hundred people all joined purely for the mission and will flag anyone who seems to not be acting for it [anthropic-co-founder-building-claude-code-lessons-from-gpt-3 @ 17:49](https://youtu.be/JdT78t1Offo?t=1069)
- Anthropic bet fully on the API for a long time on the theory that outside startups would build better products than they could, until Claude Code (an internal tool for their own engineers) surprisingly outperformed other products in the market [anthropic-co-founder-building-claude-code-lessons-from-gpt-3 @ 26:36](https://youtu.be/JdT78t1Offo?t=1596)
- Claude Code's success is credited to treating Claude itself as a user of the product, not just developers, which shaped how tools and context were built to help Claude work effectively [anthropic-co-founder-building-claude-code-lessons-from-gpt-3 @ 27:08](https://youtu.be/JdT78t1Offo?t=1628)
- Anthropic avoids building a team dedicated to gaming published benchmarks, relying instead on internal benchmarks and heavy internal dogfooding to accelerate their own engineers, which Brown thinks is the biggest factor in why founders prefer Claude for coding [anthropic-co-founder-building-claude-code-lessons-from-gpt-3 @ 24:11](https://youtu.be/JdT78t1Offo?t=1451)
- Anthropic deliberately uses GPUs, TPUs, and Trainium simultaneously, splitting performance engineering effort across all three, to soak up extra compute capacity and match the right chips to the right training or inference jobs [anthropic-co-founder-building-claude-code-lessons-from-gpt-3 @ 32:57](https://youtu.be/JdT78t1Offo?t=1977)
