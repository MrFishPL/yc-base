# Q&A with YC at the Female Founders Conference 2016 — Compiled Truth

YC partners field audience questions on who should apply to YC, what YC looks for in founders and teams, solo founders, and what has surprised them most about running YC.

**Key ideas (with timestamps):**
- There is no such thing as a company being "too early" or "too late" for YC — even the fellowship program exists for idea/prototype-stage founders [q-a-with-yc-at-the-female-founders-conference-2016 @ 00:52](https://youtu.be/FI9eyGXsD5s?t=52)
- YC looks for teams that show determination, can work together, and can get things done, plus founders solving a problem they personally have as domain experts [q-a-with-yc-at-the-female-founders-conference-2016 @ 01:46](https://youtu.be/FI9eyGXsD5s?t=106)
- YC funded Gobble even though the founder was in the "trough of sorrow" because the team was thoughtful and the general idea (prepared food delivery) was clearly onto something, even if the exact approach needed adjusting [q-a-with-yc-at-the-female-founders-conference-2016 @ 04:13](https://youtu.be/FI9eyGXsD5s?t=253)
- A strong signal of founder perseverance is having stuck with a startup (even through pivots) for a long time, since that shows real cockroach-like dedication [q-a-with-yc-at-the-female-founders-conference-2016 @ 06:17](https://youtu.be/FI9eyGXsD5s?t=377)
- In interviews, being able to explain your business clearly and succinctly signals the founder has thought it through; failing to do so in a 10-minute interview signals the opposite [q-a-with-yc-at-the-female-founders-conference-2016 @ 07:54](https://youtu.be/FI9eyGXsD5s?t=474)
- YC does fund solo founders, but a partner cautioned against tacking on a co-founder just to fill a perceived gap or check a box, since a bad co-founder fit can be more destructive than being solo [q-a-with-yc-at-the-female-founders-conference-2016 @ 10:56](https://youtu.be/FI9eyGXsD5s?t=656)
- Founder breakups are described as heartbreaking and often unrelated to the business itself — just two or three people who can't make the relationship work, which can kill the company [q-a-with-yc-at-the-female-founders-conference-2016 @ 12:15](https://youtu.be/FI9eyGXsD5s?t=735)
- Jessica Livingston says what surprised her most about YC is how broken even hugely successful, press-celebrated startups often are behind the scenes [q-a-with-yc-at-the-female-founders-conference-2016 @ 15:08](https://youtu.be/FI9eyGXsD5s?t=908)
