# The Finance Startup Bringing Agentic AI to Wall Street — Compiled Truth

Model ML founders Ariel and Chaz Englander describe building an agentic "AI workspace" for financial services, and explain how 2025 marked the shift from AI pilots to real contracts at top investment banks and private equity firms.

- Model ML rebuilt the office suite (Word, PowerPoint, Excel equivalents) on top of an agentic system connected to a firm's files, emails, CRM, data vendors, and internal data, calling this a "cognitive architecture" [the-finance-startup-bringing-agentic-ai-to-wall-street @ 01:13](https://youtu.be/lqokpIme47A?t=73)

- 2024 was a year of testing and proof-of-concepts for financial firms, while 2025 became the year those pilots converted into real, high-value contracts [the-finance-startup-bringing-agentic-ai-to-wall-street @ 09:12](https://youtu.be/lqokpIme47A?t=552)
