# How Surbhi Sarna Built A $275M Biotech Company From Nothing — Compiled Truth

Surbhi Sarna recounts founding Nvision Medical after her own ovarian cyst scare, overcoming investor bias and rejection to raise funding, and ultimately selling the company to Boston Scientific for $275M before becoming a YC group partner.

- She warns that a common founder failure mode is falling in love with an idea without talking to customers first, which is "the downfall of a lot of founders" [how-surbhi-sarna-built-a-275m-biotech-company-from-nothing @ 04:25](https://youtu.be/R2fAYn8R318?t=265)
