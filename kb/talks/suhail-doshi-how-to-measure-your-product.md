# Suhail Doshi - How to Measure Your Product — Compiled Truth

Mixpanel co-founder Suhail Doshi argues that early-stage measurement should stay radically simple: track whether people understand your product, whether they can get started easily, and whether they come back — and resist the urge to build 25-metric dashboards.

**Key ideas (with timestamps):**
- Growth reduces to a simple funnel formula — visits, signups, meaningful actions, return visits, and referrals — and these five levers are enough to diagnose almost anything going wrong [suhail-doshi-how-to-measure-your-product @ 04:46](https://youtu.be/MABmQhOlmJA?t=286)
- Companies of all sizes make the mistake of tracking too many metrics (Doshi once had 25 sub-metrics on paper), which causes decision paralysis rather than clarity [suhail-doshi-how-to-measure-your-product @ 06:12](https://youtu.be/MABmQhOlmJA?t=372)
- The simplest signal that people understand your product is whether they bother to sign up at all; for B2B, clicks on the pricing page are a strong secondary signal [suhail-doshi-how-to-measure-your-product @ 13:30](https://youtu.be/MABmQhOlmJA?t=810)
- Mixpanel deliberately skipped building "forgot password" for 12-18 months, ruthlessly prioritizing only what would move signups and retention [suhail-doshi-how-to-measure-your-product @ 16:59](https://youtu.be/MABmQhOlmJA?t=1019)
- Email/SMS confirmation steps cause enormous drop-off (often 60-70%), so founders should avoid adding fraud-prevention friction before they actually have a fraud problem [suhail-doshi-how-to-measure-your-product @ 21:15](https://youtu.be/MABmQhOlmJA?t=1275)
- The "shark fin" pattern — viral user acquisition followed by a collapse — happens when retention is ignored during a growth spike, and reactivation campaigns rarely recover those lost users [suhail-doshi-how-to-measure-your-product @ 23:21](https://youtu.be/MABmQhOlmJA?t=1401)
- Monthly active users is becoming as weak a vanity metric as total registered users once was; daily active users is a harsher, more honest signal, and DAU correlated strongly with reduced churn at Mixpanel [suhail-doshi-how-to-measure-your-product @ 26:33](https://youtu.be/MABmQhOlmJA?t=1593)
- A seemingly small 7% monthly revenue churn compounds to roughly 58% annual revenue loss, which founders consistently underestimate [suhail-doshi-how-to-measure-your-product @ 28:52](https://youtu.be/MABmQhOlmJA?t=1732)
- With under 50 users, the right move is to just talk to them directly (Doshi badgered ~11 early customers over chat) rather than trying to run statistically rigorous experiments [suhail-doshi-how-to-measure-your-product @ 31:08](https://youtu.be/MABmQhOlmJA?t=1868)
- He recommends picking one North Star metric you'd "bet the company on," committing to it for at least six months, plus three to five secondary metrics — and no more [suhail-doshi-how-to-measure-your-product @ 34:35](https://youtu.be/MABmQhOlmJA?t=2075)
