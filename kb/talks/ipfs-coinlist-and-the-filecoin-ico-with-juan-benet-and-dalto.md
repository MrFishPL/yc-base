# IPFS, CoinList, and the Filecoin ICO with Juan Benet and Dalton Caldwell — Compiled Truth

Juan Benet explains why he founded Protocol Labs to build IPFS and Filecoin outside of academia or Google, and discusses with Dalton Caldwell why peer-to-peer's "winter" ended, how token-funded protocol development can replace VC and Bell-Labs-style funding for long-horizon infrastructure research, and why UX (not ideology) determines whether decentralized systems get adopted.

**Key ideas (with timestamps):**
- Juan started Protocol Labs because building an ambitious peer-to-peer infrastructure project didn't fit either path available to him: a university would have forced him to publish small incremental results, and Google's business incentives run counter to decentralizing control of data [ipfs-coinlist-and-the-filecoin-ico-with-juan-benet-and-dalto @ 01:21](https://youtu.be/iUVLuXjPAfg?t=81)
- IPFS is a peer-to-peer, content-addressed protocol for distributing any kind of data, built on the thesis that "it's time for the internet to move from location addressing to content addressing" [ipfs-coinlist-and-the-filecoin-ico-with-juan-benet-and-dalto @ 04:04](https://youtu.be/iUVLuXjPAfg?t=244)
- Filecoin creates a currency to mediate a two-sided storage market (people with spare disk space vs. people who need storage) and uses a new "proof of replication" consensus mechanism so mining power comes from verified storage rather than wasted hashing [ipfs-coinlist-and-the-filecoin-ico-with-juan-benet-and-dalto @ 09:50](https://youtu.be/iUVLuXjPAfg?t=590)
- From his earlier peer-to-peer social networking startup, Juan learned that usability trumps the technical elegance of peer-to-peer models — Dropbox won not on decentralization but on nailing UX, and centralized YouTube beat peer-to-peer alternatives outright [ipfs-coinlist-and-the-filecoin-ico-with-juan-benet-and-dalto @ 13:45](https://youtu.be/iUVLuXjPAfg?t=825)
- A "peer-to-peer winter" from roughly 2003-2009 was caused by copyright infringement being the dominant use case, immature digital currencies, hardware/bandwidth limits, and cloud-infrastructure investment pulling research talent toward centralized systems [ipfs-coinlist-and-the-filecoin-ico-with-juan-benet-and-dalto @ 15:09](https://youtu.be/iUVLuXjPAfg?t=909)
- Ethereum's breakthrough wasn't a consumer product — it made it cheap to create and verify financial instruments and smart contracts, opening a category of use cases (e.g., trivial flight-delay insurance contracts) that Silicon Valley initially missed because it was fixated on consumer needs [ipfs-coinlist-and-the-filecoin-ico-with-juan-benet-and-dalto @ 30:29](https://youtu.be/iUVLuXjPAfg?t=1829)
- VC funding is structurally unsuited to long-horizon, high-risk infrastructure research because it's tuned for ~10-year returns requiring strong signals of adoption within 2-3 years, leaving a funding gap for foundational protocol work that used to be filled by organizations like Bell Labs [ipfs-coinlist-and-the-filecoin-ico-with-juan-benet-and-dalto @ 48:05](https://youtu.be/iUVLuXjPAfg?t=2885)
- Protocol Labs' business model solves that funding gap by creating a native token tied to the protocol's value and using a portion of it to fund the people building the protocol, letting them focus on the protocol layer without building a product on top [ipfs-coinlist-and-the-filecoin-ico-with-juan-benet-and-dalto @ 1:02:32](https://youtu.be/iUVLuXjPAfg?t=3752)
- UX remains the biggest blocker to blockchain/decentralized systems reaching mainstream consumer use — projects like OpenBazaar have had to build both the entire peer-to-peer stack and separate polished UX layers (web and mobile) from scratch, which is a massive undertaking [ipfs-coinlist-and-the-filecoin-ico-with-juan-benet-and-dalto @ 43:09](https://youtu.be/iUVLuXjPAfg?t=2589)
- Juan approaches evaluating crypto projects like startup investing — thinking deeply about whether the underlying value being created is real over a 2-10 year horizon, rather than day-trading on hype, since it's currently "really hard to lose money" in the ICO honeymoon period [ipfs-coinlist-and-the-filecoin-ico-with-juan-benet-and-dalto @ 1:08:08](https://youtu.be/iUVLuXjPAfg?t=4088)
