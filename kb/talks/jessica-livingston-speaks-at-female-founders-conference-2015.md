# Jessica Livingston Speaks at Female Founders Conference 2015 — Compiled Truth

Jessica Livingston explains that YC's real innovation, alongside its investment structure, was a deliberately curated culture built on judging founders' character, and that this "family" atmosphere is what no imitator has been able to replicate.

**Key ideas (with timestamps):**
- 23% of startups that applied to YC that batch had a female founder, and 23% of accepted startups did too, showing accepted founders directly mirror the applicant pool [jessica-livingston-speaks-at-female-founders-conference-2015 @ 01:17](https://youtu.be/NEp9aaQuYp4?t=77)
- While her co-founders Paul Graham, Robert Morris, and Trevor Blackwell judged technical ideas, Jessica judged people's character in interviews, and for the first seven years effectively had veto power over funding decisions [jessica-livingston-speaks-at-female-founders-conference-2015 @ 06:45](https://youtu.be/NEp9aaQuYp4?t=405)
