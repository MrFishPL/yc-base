# Elon Musk : How to Build the Future — Compiled Truth

Elon Musk describes how he picks problems by estimating usefulness times the number of people affected, why he pursued SpaceX and Tesla despite believing the odds of success were low, and why he believes AI must be democratized rather than controlled by any one company.

**Key ideas (with timestamps):**
- Something doesn't need to change the world to be good — a small amount of good delivered to a large number of people, or a large amount of good delivered to a few, both matter [elon-musk-how-to-build-the-future @ 00:18](https://youtu.be/tnBQmEqBCY0?t=18)
- Musk ranks AI as the single biggest near-term item likely to affect humanity's future, followed by genetics (solving genetic disease) and a high-bandwidth brain-computer interface [elon-musk-how-to-build-the-future @ 01:01](https://youtu.be/tnBQmEqBCY0?t=61)
- To judge how useful a pursuit is, estimate the utility delta versus the current state of the art times how many people it would affect [elon-musk-how-to-build-the-future @ 04:55](https://youtu.be/tnBQmEqBCY0?t=295)
- Musk started SpaceX believing the odds of success were less than 10%, accepting he would likely lose everything but that failing forward might still move the industry ahead [elon-musk-how-to-build-the-future @ 08:54](https://youtu.be/tnBQmEqBCY0?t=534)
- He founded SpaceX not for risk-adjusted return but because he concluded rocket technology would otherwise stay stuck, citing that aerospace incumbents only wanted incremental improvements to old technology [elon-musk-how-to-build-the-future @ 06:20](https://youtu.be/tnBQmEqBCY0?t=380)
- Technology does not automatically improve — it only gets better when people work hard to improve it, and can regress, as shown by lost technical knowledge in ancient Egypt and Rome [elon-musk-how-to-build-the-future @ 07:03](https://youtu.be/tnBQmEqBCY0?t=423)
- Musk says he does feel fear strongly but lets the importance of a goal drive him to act anyway, and that accepting the probabilities of failure (fatalism) can diminish fear [elon-musk-how-to-build-the-future @ 08:13](https://youtu.be/tnBQmEqBCY0?t=493)
- He believes AI must be democratized so that no single company or small group controls advanced AI technology, since concentrated control is unstable and could be stolen or misused [elon-musk-how-to-build-the-future @ 11:34](https://youtu.be/tnBQmEqBCY0?t=694)
- OpenAI was created specifically to help democratize AI technology so it doesn't concentrate in the hands of a few [elon-musk-how-to-build-the-future @ 12:43](https://youtu.be/tnBQmEqBCY0?t=763)
- Despite running SpaceX and Tesla, Musk says roughly 80% of his time is spent on engineering and design rather than business or media activities [elon-musk-how-to-build-the-future @ 16:18](https://youtu.be/tnBQmEqBCY0?t=978)
