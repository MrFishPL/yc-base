# Why Now Is The Best Time To Build In Crypto — Compiled Truth

Base founder Jesse Pollak argues crypto has entered a "golden age" for builders because cheap fast chains, mature stablecoins, simplified wallets, and new regulatory clarity have finally removed the infrastructure and legal barriers that stalled the last decade of crypto startups.

**Key ideas (with timestamps):**
- Four converging shifts unlocked this moment: chain costs dropped from dollars to fractions of a cent per transaction, regulatory clarity emerged (Genius Act, Clarity Act), stablecoins matured into real programmable dollars, and wallets got simple enough to embed in normal UX [why-now-is-the-best-time-to-build-in-crypto @ 12:07](https://youtu.be/Pdne9xaRLUc?t=727)
- Previously, early-stage crypto founders often spent more on lawyers than engineers because of regulatory uncertainty, which made teams unfundable and forced them into legal work that should have been someone else's job [why-now-is-the-best-time-to-build-in-crypto @ 09:12](https://youtu.be/Pdne9xaRLUc?t=552)
- Stablecoins took years to take off (it took a while for USDC to reach a billion in issuance) but now represent almost $200 billion in the market, primarily by giving people outside the US programmable access to dollars they couldn't otherwise hold [why-now-is-the-best-time-to-build-in-crypto @ 13:51](https://youtu.be/Pdne9xaRLUc?t=831)
- A major emerging opportunity is local-currency stablecoins (Brazilian real, Nigerian naira, Indonesian rupiah) that let countries capture stablecoin benefits without their economies becoming fully dollarized [why-now-is-the-best-time-to-build-in-crypto @ 17:05](https://youtu.be/Pdne9xaRLUc?t=1025)
- Coinbase and Shopify rewrote a commerce payments system with escrow, taxes, fees, and chargebacks from millions of lines of legacy code into roughly a thousand lines of on-chain smart contract code, over a nine-month build [why-now-is-the-best-time-to-build-in-crypto @ 21:53](https://youtu.be/Pdne9xaRLUc?t=1313)
