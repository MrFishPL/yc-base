# Tom Blomfield: How I Created Two Billion-Dollar Fintech Startups — Compiled Truth

Tom Blomfield recounts founding GoCardless and Monzo, describing how a tarpit bill-splitting idea pivoted into a B2B payments company, how Monzo grew to a million customers via word of mouth, and the extreme emotional toll of nearly losing the bank during COVID.

**Key ideas (with timestamps):**
- Blomfield's team pivoted from a "classic tarpit idea" (a bill-splitting app) into a much better B2B payment idea after YC showed them the bar for execution was far higher than they thought [tom-blomfield-how-i-created-two-billion-dollar-fintech-start @ 03:06](https://youtu.be/QKPgBAnbc10?t=186)
- He left GoCardless after three years because the problem — collecting money for small businesses — wasn't close enough to his heart, even though he loved the business and colleagues [tom-blomfield-how-i-created-two-billion-dollar-fintech-start @ 04:03](https://youtu.be/QKPgBAnbc10?t=243)
- Monzo launched a prototype bank on a prepaid debit card in three or four months, leaning on YC lessons to "launch early and talk to users, do things that don't scale" [tom-blomfield-how-i-created-two-billion-dollar-fintech-start @ 05:10](https://youtu.be/QKPgBAnbc10?t=310)
- Monzo grew from zero to a million customers in about two and a half years without spending anything on advertising, driven entirely by word of mouth from a delightful card-tap experience [tom-blomfield-how-i-created-two-billion-dollar-fintech-start @ 05:48](https://youtu.be/QKPgBAnbc10?t=348)
- Taking on a big, ambitious vision (starting a bank from scratch) attracted better talent, press coverage, and excited customers, which he credits as part of why Monzo succeeded [tom-blomfield-how-i-created-two-billion-dollar-fintech-start @ 06:40](https://youtu.be/QKPgBAnbc10?t=400)
- During COVID, Monzo's revenue dropped about 50%, a $10-100 million funding round was pulled 72 hours before closing, and regulators told the company to start preparing to wind down [tom-blomfield-how-i-created-two-billion-dollar-fintech-start @ 09:13](https://youtu.be/QKPgBAnbc10?t=553)
- The pressure led Blomfield to stop functioning and eventually step back from the CEO role, describing it as giving up a huge part of his identity in order to survive [tom-blomfield-how-i-created-two-billion-dollar-fintech-start @ 10:33](https://youtu.be/QKPgBAnbc10?t=633)
- After stepping back, a new CEO and COO alongside his co-founder/CTO carried Monzo to more than 8 million UK customers (15-20% of the adult population) and profitability [tom-blomfield-how-i-created-two-billion-dollar-fintech-start @ 11:25](https://youtu.be/QKPgBAnbc10?t=685)
- After taking a year off, he found renewed energy advising founders as an angel investor, making about 75 investments in nine months before joining YC as a partner [tom-blomfield-how-i-created-two-billion-dollar-fintech-start @ 12:42](https://youtu.be/QKPgBAnbc10?t=762)
