# How to Build a Self-Improving Company with AI — Compiled Truth

Companies traditionally organized like Roman legions, with humans as the conduit for information, can be reimagined as recursive, self-improving AI loops that keep getting better even while people sleep.

**Key ideas (with timestamps):**
- Most companies are organized hierarchically like Roman legions, with humans passing information up and down the chain, and AI breaks that assumption [how-to-build-a-self-improving-company-with-ai @ 00:41](https://youtu.be/X_JsIHUfUjc?t=41)
- Thinking of AI as just a productivity copilot (20% more efficient engineers) is a broken framing that just bolts a more powerful engine onto the old way of working [how-to-build-a-self-improving-company-with-ai @ 01:02](https://youtu.be/X_JsIHUfUjc?t=62)
- A self-improving AI loop has a sensor layer (customer emails, support tickets, telemetry), a policy layer, a tool layer, a quality gate, and a learning mechanism that feeds back to the top [how-to-build-a-self-improving-company-with-ai @ 02:48](https://youtu.be/X_JsIHUfUjc?t=168)
