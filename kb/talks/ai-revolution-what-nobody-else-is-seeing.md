The Lightcone hosts recap a 300-person YC AI founder retreat, arguing that AI has expanded the universe of viable businesses and pushed growth expectations, technical hiring bars, and enterprise sales dynamics to unprecedented levels.

- Unlike prior tech cycles (cloud, mobile) where enterprise decision-makers resisted change, AI is the first cycle where "everyone is saying yes," creating unprecedented enterprise demand [ai-revolution-what-nobody-else-is-seeing @ 05:05](https://youtu.be/0LMK5JYkB94?t=305)

- Technical founders who aren't strong at sales are winning big enterprise contracts against 10-15 competitors simply because building a product that reliably works is so hard [ai-revolution-what-nobody-else-is-seeing @ 06:36](https://youtu.be/0LMK5JYkB94?t=396)
