# How To Build Generative AI Models Like OpenAI's Sora — Compiled Truth

The Lightcone hosts react to OpenAI's Sora and show how several YC batch companies built their own foundation models with only ~$500k by hacking data, compute, and expertise constraints rather than needing OpenAI-scale resources.

**Key ideas (with timestamps):**
- YC companies have built working foundation models during a single batch using just the standard $500,000 YC investment, proving you don't need billions of dollars and a data-center full of GPUs to compete [how-to-build-generative-ai-models-like-openai-s-sora @ 09:20](https://youtu.be/fmI_OciHV_8?t=560)
- Sora is technically a combination of a transformer model (typically used for text) and a diffusion model (used in DALL-E/Midjourney) plus a temporal component, trained on "spacetime patches" that act like tokens for video [how-to-build-generative-ai-models-like-openai-s-sora @ 05:22](https://youtu.be/fmI_OciHV_8?t=322)
- Infinity AI built a deepfake video model by compressing training data to low-resolution video and leveraging YC's dedicated Azure GPU cluster to iterate 100x faster than they could otherwise [how-to-build-generative-ai-models-like-openai-s-sora @ 12:13](https://youtu.be/fmI_OciHV_8?t=733)
- Sonauto, built by 21-year-old new-college-grad founders with no ML PhDs, trained a text-to-song model in about two months just by teaching themselves from papers [how-to-build-generative-ai-models-like-openai-s-sora @ 14:08](https://youtu.be/fmI_OciHV_8?t=848)
- Metalware, founded by ex-SpaceX hardware engineers with no AI background, got away with using less but higher-quality data (scanned hardware textbooks) and a smaller model (GPT-2.5-scale, ~1B parameters) to build a hardware co-pilot [how-to-build-generative-ai-models-like-openai-s-sora @ 16:14](https://youtu.be/fmI_OciHV_8?t=974)
- Synthetic data, once controversial ("like a mosquito drinking its own blood"), turned out to work well, as shown by Pear building a coding co-pilot on synthetic programming-competition data [how-to-build-generative-ai-models-like-openai-s-sora @ 19:49](https://youtu.be/fmI_OciHV_8?t=1189)
- Because these models are general-purpose function approximators, teams are applying the same spacetime-patch approach beyond video, e.g., Cradle Bio for protein/drug design and Piramidal for modeling EEG brain signals [how-to-build-generative-ai-models-like-openai-s-sora @ 24:24](https://youtu.be/fmI_OciHV_8?t=1464)
- Startups worried about being run over by foundation-model giants can instead train their own specialized model, as Playground (founded by Suhail Doshi, who taught himself AI by locking himself away reading papers for a month after pivoting) did to compete with Midjourney [how-to-build-generative-ai-models-like-openai-s-sora @ 30:27](https://youtu.be/fmI_OciHV_8?t=1827)
- The field is new enough that spending six to nine months reading every paper and meeting the people in the space can put a founder on the cutting edge in relatively short order [how-to-build-generative-ai-models-like-openai-s-sora @ 32:23](https://youtu.be/fmI_OciHV_8?t=1943)
