# GPT-4.5 = Big Model Energy | YC Decoded — Compiled Truth

A YC Decoded explainer on OpenAI's GPT-4.5 launch, arguing it is a large, expensive, non-reasoning model whose gains are in emotional intelligence and creativity rather than benchmarks, and a bridge toward future models that unify pretraining scale with reasoning.
