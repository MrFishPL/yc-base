# Adora Cheung Speaks at Female Founders Conference 2015 — Compiled Truth

Homejoy CEO Adora Cheung argues that startups shouldn't scale anything until they've confirmed product-market fit, a large opportunity, and good timing, then walks through concrete tactics for scaling product, team, and yourself.
