# How Amplitude Went From Skeptics to All In on AI — Compiled Truth

Amplitude CEO Spencer Skates describes how a decade-old SaaS company reoriented itself around AI, arguing that the shift required bottoms-up technology fluency rather than top-down mandates, and that surviving as a founder or company through any transformation depends on staying anchored to a clear underlying "why."

**Key ideas (with timestamps):**
- Incumbent companies struggle to build AI products because engineers and leadership are often skeptical or "grumpy," slowing adoption even as investors and executives pressure them to have an "AI strategy" [how-amplitude-went-from-skeptics-to-all-in-on-ai @ 01:18](https://youtu.be/t8co94HS6tY?t=78)
- Amplitude's real AI push began in earnest around October 2024, catalyzed by hiring engineering leader Wade Chambers and acquiring the YC company Command AI [how-amplitude-went-from-skeptics-to-all-in-on-ai @ 04:25](https://youtu.be/t8co94HS6tY?t=265)
- A company-wide "AI week" in June 2025, where leaders demoed AI tools live before training and then running a hackathon, was the key inflection point for organizational buy-in [how-amplitude-went-from-skeptics-to-all-in-on-ai @ 08:57](https://youtu.be/t8co94HS6tY?t=537)
- Because AI model capabilities are "jagged," you can't just ask customers what they want the way classic SaaS discovery works — you need a technology-first understanding of what's actually possible [how-amplitude-went-from-skeptics-to-all-in-on-ai @ 10:40](https://youtu.be/t8co94HS6tY?t=640)
- Amplitude ran two reorganizations of its product/eng org within a year, moving out people not adapting to AI, while also acquiring several YC founding teams (Craftful, Anari, June) to accelerate the shift [how-amplitude-went-from-skeptics-to-all-in-on-ai @ 15:36](https://youtu.be/t8co94HS6tY?t=936)

- Fast-to-commoditize AI features like "AI visibility" are only valuable as lead generation; the real defensible business has to be built downstream of them [how-amplitude-went-from-skeptics-to-all-in-on-ai @ 24:29](https://youtu.be/t8co94HS6tY?t=1469)
- Founders need to get crystal clear on their personal underlying motivation ("why") because that's what lets them keep going through the multi-year point where quitting would be the rational choice [how-amplitude-went-from-skeptics-to-all-in-on-ai @ 34:32](https://youtu.be/t8co94HS6tY?t=2072)
