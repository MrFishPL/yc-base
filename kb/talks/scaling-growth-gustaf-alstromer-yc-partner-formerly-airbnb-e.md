# Scaling Growth | Gustaf Alstromer & Ed Baker — Compiled Truth

Former growth leaders from Facebook, Uber, and Airbnb trace how growth teams evolved from viral social hacking to disciplined, metrics-driven organizations spanning product, engineering, data science, and paid marketing.

**Key ideas (with timestamps):**
- At Facebook's scale, retention and engagement became bigger drivers of the monthly-active-user Northstar metric than new user acquisition, so the growth team shifted focus to friending and re-engagement [scaling-growth-gustaf-alstromer-yc-partner-formerly-airbnb-e @ 07:10](https://youtu.be/bYf43D4nUPk?t=430)
- A well-formed growth team should combine five functions working together: product, engineering, analytics, design, and marketing [scaling-growth-gustaf-alstromer-yc-partner-formerly-airbnb-e @ 07:53](https://youtu.be/bYf43D4nUPk?t=473)
- A Northstar metric is the one number the whole growth team is trying to move; if your work doesn't ultimately grow that number, you're probably working on the wrong thing [scaling-growth-gustaf-alstromer-yc-partner-formerly-airbnb-e @ 11:57](https://youtu.be/bYf43D4nUPk?t=717)
- Uber chose weekly trips as its Northstar metric instead of monthly active riders because trips capture both sides of the two-sided marketplace (riders and drivers) [scaling-growth-gustaf-alstromer-yc-partner-formerly-airbnb-e @ 12:34](https://youtu.be/bYf43D4nUPk?t=754)
- As free organic growth channels shrink, most good growth teams should invest more in paid marketing, integrating it tightly with product and engineering [scaling-growth-gustaf-alstromer-yc-partner-formerly-airbnb-e @ 16:17](https://youtu.be/bYf43D4nUPk?t=977)
- The single most important driver of new growth channels is still having a great product with product-market fit that people want to share with friends [scaling-growth-gustaf-alstromer-yc-partner-formerly-airbnb-e @ 19:14](https://youtu.be/bYf43D4nUPk?t=1154)
- Growth teams at Facebook regularly ran an "experiment review" where the audience guessed which variant won before reveal, and disagreement was common, underscoring how unpredictable product decisions are [scaling-growth-gustaf-alstromer-yc-partner-formerly-airbnb-e @ 25:10](https://youtu.be/bYf43D4nUPk?t=1510)
- In Japan, simply renaming "invite your friends" to "announce to your friends" turned Facebook's least-viral country into its most viral, showing how small copy tweaks tied to cultural norms can massively change sharing behavior [scaling-growth-gustaf-alstromer-yc-partner-formerly-airbnb-e @ 26:09](https://youtu.be/bYf43D4nUPk?t=1569)
- A simple growth tip: don't automatically log users out after a period of inactivity, since there's little benefit and it hurts retention [scaling-growth-gustaf-alstromer-yc-partner-formerly-airbnb-e @ 25:10](https://youtu.be/bYf43D4nUPk?t=1510)
