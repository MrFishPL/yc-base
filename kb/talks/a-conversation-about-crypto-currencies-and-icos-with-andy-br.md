# A Conversation About Crypto-currencies and ICOs with Andy Bromberg — Compiled Truth

CoinList co-founder Andy Bromberg explains what tokens and ICOs actually are, why they're a poor fit for almost every startup today, and how token economics differ from traditional equity fundraising.

**Key ideas (with timestamps):**
- CoinList has fielded over 2,500 inbound requests to run token sales but has only publicly worked with five, because the overwhelming majority of proposed token sales are not a good fit or are outright scams [a-conversation-about-crypto-currencies-and-icos-with-andy-br @ 32:17](https://youtu.be/q_NbKjVc2l8?t=1937)
- More money was raised via ICOs than through venture capital in 2018 (roughly $15B), which Bromberg reads as a warning sign rather than a sign of health given how young and undiligenced the industry is [a-conversation-about-crypto-currencies-and-icos-with-andy-br @ 32:54](https://youtu.be/q_NbKjVc2l8?t=1974)
- Tokens are a good fit only where parties don't trust each other and don't want to trust a central intermediary either — Airbnb, where users already trust Airbnb as an intermediary, is a bad candidate for a token today [a-conversation-about-crypto-currencies-and-icos-with-andy-br @ 40:04](https://youtu.be/q_NbKjVc2l8?t=2404)
- Filecoin's token design uses staking (storage providers put tokens at risk) and a network of verifiers doing spot-checks so that no trusted intermediary is needed to guarantee files are stored correctly [a-conversation-about-crypto-currencies-and-icos-with-andy-br @ 27:40](https://youtu.be/q_NbKjVc2l8?t=1660)
- Unlike equity investors, token holders have no formal relationship or ongoing alignment with the company, so a pumping token price can trigger dumping that hurts the company even though the investor has "won" [a-conversation-about-crypto-currencies-and-icos-with-andy-br @ 43:13](https://youtu.be/q_NbKjVc2l8?t=2593)
- The ICO market is converging toward venture-style structures, with vesting schedules, lockups, and staged raises replacing the early "one monster token sale" model [a-conversation-about-crypto-currencies-and-icos-with-andy-br @ 45:28](https://youtu.be/q_NbKjVc2l8?t=2728)
- Good investors are identified by alignment with the founder's actual goals, how positive the relationship feels even during hard diligence conversations, and reference checks — calling other founders they've backed before committing [a-conversation-about-crypto-currencies-and-icos-with-andy-br @ 46:36](https://youtu.be/q_NbKjVc2l8?t=2796)
- Bromberg's first company, Sidewire, built a platform for expert political discussion and had strong "supply" (about 1,000 senators, reporters, and analysts engaging) but could never retain a "demand" side of readers, and was wound down in 2017 after concluding the business could never be venture-scale [a-conversation-about-crypto-currencies-and-icos-with-andy-br @ 12:08](https://youtu.be/q_NbKjVc2l8?t=728)
- Building a habit is easiest with something bite-sized; Sidewire's failure to retain users partly stemmed from asking people to read long expert conversations rather than something small and quick like a tweet [a-conversation-about-crypto-currencies-and-icos-with-andy-br @ 17:11](https://youtu.be/q_NbKjVc2l8?t=1031)
- Security-token offerings (asset-backed tokens representing equity, real estate, etc.) are a distinct category from protocol tokens, and CoinList sees itself as infrastructure that can support either without it being a "pivot" [a-conversation-about-crypto-currencies-and-icos-with-andy-br @ 51:16](https://youtu.be/q_NbKjVc2l8?t=3076)
