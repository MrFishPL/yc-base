# The Best Way To Launch Your Startup | Startup School — Compiled Truth

Launching is not a single perfect moment but a continuous practice: ship early, launch through many channels, and keep re-launching until you find users who really love you.

**Key ideas (with timestamps):**
- Most founders overthink their first launch; the right time to launch is ASAP because founders have theoretical, unproven notions about their product until it's tested in the wild [the-best-way-to-launch-your-startup-startup-school @ 00:31](https://youtu.be/u36A-YTxiOw?t=31)
- If a launch flops, that's not a reason to quit — Airbnb launched three times before it started getting real traction [the-best-way-to-launch-your-startup-startup-school @ 02:23](https://youtu.be/u36A-YTxiOw?t=143)
- Paul Buchheit's principle: it's better to make a few people really happy than to make a lot of people semi-happy [the-best-way-to-launch-your-startup-startup-school @ 02:45](https://youtu.be/u36A-YTxiOw?t=165)
- A clear, succinct one-sentence pitch requires clarity of vision and is the foundation for word-of-mouth growth [the-best-way-to-launch-your-startup-startup-school @ 03:47](https://youtu.be/u36A-YTxiOw?t=227)
- Lead pitches with "what" not "why" — state the company and what it does before the backstory, as in Pave's "lets companies plan communicate and Benchmark your compensation in real time" [the-best-way-to-launch-your-startup-startup-school @ 05:11](https://youtu.be/u36A-YTxiOw?t=311)
- Avoid meaningless marketing jargon in descriptions — a phrase like "know-how and Synergy platform" conveys zero informational content about what you actually build [the-best-way-to-launch-your-startup-startup-school @ 06:59](https://youtu.be/u36A-YTxiOw?t=419)
- The "X for Y" construction only works when X is a household name, it's clear why Y wants X, and Y is a huge market [the-best-way-to-launch-your-startup-startup-school @ 09:29](https://youtu.be/u36A-YTxiOw?t=569)
- Every founder should do a silent launch during startup school: a simple landing page with a domain, company name, short description, and a call to action [the-best-way-to-launch-your-startup-startup-school @ 12:01](https://youtu.be/u36A-YTxiOw?t=721)
- After a silent launch, do a friends-and-family launch quickly (Reddit was first shared among founders of YC's first batch) but don't linger there too long since friends and family aren't ideal target users [the-best-way-to-launch-your-startup-startup-school @ 12:40](https://youtu.be/u36A-YTxiOw?t=760)
- DoorDash's founders talked to 200 small business owners, discovered the real delivery pain point from a macaron store manager named Chloe, and built their MVP in a few hours once they had that insight [the-best-way-to-launch-your-startup-startup-school @ 13:40](https://youtu.be/u36A-YTxiOw?t=820)
- Launching through online communities (Bookface, Hacker News' Show HN) is a favorite channel — Robinhood's simple waitlist landing page hit #1 on HN and got 10,000 signups the first day [the-best-way-to-launch-your-startup-startup-school @ 15:01](https://youtu.be/u36A-YTxiOw?t=901)
