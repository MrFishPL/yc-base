# How to Design Hardware Products with Hosain Rahman — Compiled Truth

Jawbone's founder walks through how the company builds "experiences" by treating hardware, software, and data as three equal disciplines and running every product through a disciplined explore-validate-concept-plan-develop-launch pipeline anchored on a "why" framework.

**Key ideas (with timestamps):**
- Jawbone sees itself uniquely playing at the intersection of hardware, software, and data as three equal pillars that must work together to power a connected experience [how-to-design-hardware-products-with-hosain-rahman-how-to-st @ 05:02](https://youtu.be/9u6-BL-Fskc?t=302)
- Products move through a deliberate pipeline: unbridled exploration, early validation, concept, heavy planning, development, launch, then iterate [how-to-design-hardware-products-with-hosain-rahman-how-to-st @ 07:40](https://youtu.be/9u6-BL-Fskc?t=460)
- In the exploration phase the bar to advance an idea is framed like an angel investment: "would I give this guy 50 grand to go explore this?" [how-to-design-hardware-products-with-hosain-rahman-how-to-st @ 11:05](https://youtu.be/9u6-BL-Fskc?t=665)
- Every product is built around a "why" — the specific user problem being solved that, once solved, people can't live without [how-to-design-hardware-products-with-hosain-rahman-how-to-st @ 19:21](https://youtu.be/9u6-BL-Fskc?t=1161)
- User research deliberately avoids asking people what they want to buy and instead asks how they behave and live, because no one can tell you what to build [how-to-design-hardware-products-with-hosain-rahman-how-to-st @ 26:02](https://youtu.be/9u6-BL-Fskc?t=1562)
- The wireless speaker market was 0% of the overall speaker market when Jambox launched in 2010 and grew to 78% of holiday speaker sales by Christmas 2013, despite focus groups likely rejecting the idea upfront [how-to-design-hardware-products-with-hosain-rahman-how-to-st @ 19:40](https://youtu.be/9u6-BL-Fskc?t=1180)
- Cross-functional "pods" combining product experience, hardware engineering, software engineering, and data people own each product theme end to end [how-to-design-hardware-products-with-hosain-rahman-how-to-st @ 18:29](https://youtu.be/9u6-BL-Fskc?t=1109)
- As the company scales, staying aligned on trade-offs requires forcing communication — e.g. a daily two-and-a-half-hour cross-team call during the Up3 sensor redesign [how-to-design-hardware-products-with-hosain-rahman-how-to-st @ 42:48](https://youtu.be/9u6-BL-Fskc?t=2568)
