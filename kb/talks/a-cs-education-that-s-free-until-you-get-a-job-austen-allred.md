# A CS Education That's Free Until You Get a Job - Austen Allred of Lambda School — Compiled Truth

Austen Allred describes Lambda School's core challenge as convincing capable but under-resourced students to take the leap into an income-share-funded education, and argues the company's real moat is its educational product, underwriting data, and hiring network rather than the ISA model itself.
