# Elon Musk On Fear — Compiled Truth

Elon Musk explains that he feels fear strongly rather than lacking it, and describes how accepting bad odds (fatalism) let him start SpaceX and Tesla anyway.

**Key ideas (with timestamps):**
- Musk says he feels fear quite strongly — he isn't fearless, he just doesn't let it stop him [elon-musk-on-fear @ 00:00](https://youtu.be/YH1txRfa0oY?t=0).
- He says when something is important enough and you believe in it enough, you do it in spite of the fear [elon-musk-on-fear @ 00:13](https://youtu.be/YH1txRfa0oY?t=13).
- He argues feeling fear is normal and something would be mentally wrong with you if you didn't feel it; you just let the importance of the goal drive you anyway [elon-musk-on-fear @ 00:35](https://youtu.be/YH1txRfa0oY?t=35).
- Musk says fatalism helps: if you accept the probabilities of failure, that diminishes fear [elon-musk-on-fear @ 00:54](https://youtu.be/YH1txRfa0oY?t=54).
- He says he thought the odds of SpaceX succeeding were less than 10%, and he accepted he would probably lose everything but that the effort could still move the ball forward for others [elon-musk-on-fear @ 00:54](https://youtu.be/YH1txRfa0oY?t=54).
- He says he thought the odds of Tesla succeeding as a company were also extremely low, going in with the same mindset [elon-musk-on-fear @ 01:17](https://youtu.be/YH1txRfa0oY?t=77).
