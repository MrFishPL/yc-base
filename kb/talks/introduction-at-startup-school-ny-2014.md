# Introduction at Startup School NY 2014 — Compiled Truth

Alexis Ohanian opens the first-ever New York Startup School by recounting YC's origins in Boston and celebrating the growing YC founder community in New York.

**Key ideas (with timestamps):**
- This is the first Startup School held in New York City, a milestone in YC's history [introduction-at-startup-school-ny-2014 @ 00:06](https://youtu.be/VuVhxwwvCXQ?t=6)
- Alexis Ohanian and his co-founder Steve Huffman (Reddit) attended the very first Startup School in Boston back in 2005, when YC had to beg people to attend a new, unproven idea [introduction-at-startup-school-ny-2014 @ 01:12](https://youtu.be/VuVhxwwvCXQ?t=72)
- Startup School intentionally brings together people who are actually doing startups rather than "scenesters" who just talk about them [introduction-at-startup-school-ny-2014 @ 01:46](https://youtu.be/VuVhxwwvCXQ?t=106)
- There are now 28 Y Combinator companies based in New York, including Amicus, Hacker School, One Month Rails, Vidible, RentHop, Weave, and Rocket Trip [introduction-at-startup-school-ny-2014 @ 02:20](https://youtu.be/VuVhxwwvCXQ?t=140)
- These NY-based YC companies did their three months in Silicon Valley to access the network, then returned to New York to grow [introduction-at-startup-school-ny-2014 @ 02:40](https://youtu.be/VuVhxwwvCXQ?t=160)
- Having toured 80 universities, Ohanian observed that student entrepreneurship is thriving, with students building, learning, and coding on their own initiative far more than nine years earlier [introduction-at-startup-school-ny-2014 @ 03:38](https://youtu.be/VuVhxwwvCXQ?t=218)
- Ohanian frames the current era as uniquely lucky: in the internet age, changing the world no longer requires opening a factory, just opening a laptop [introduction-at-startup-school-ny-2014 @ 04:20](https://youtu.be/VuVhxwwvCXQ?t=260)
