# Paul Buchheit: What traits do startups need to succeed? — Compiled Truth

Paul Buchheit argues that focus and frugality are the two traits that let underpowered startups beat large, unfocused incumbents.

**Key ideas (with timestamps):**
- Focus is a startup's most powerful weapon because big companies are spread across a thousand different things while a startup can concentrate everything it has on one point [paul-buchheit-what-traits-do-startups-need-to-succeed @ 00:05](https://youtu.be/zVY-NF6ZvEQ?t=5)
- Being underpowered in every other way, a startup's one advantage is the ability to be 100% focused on what it's doing [paul-buchheit-what-traits-do-startups-need-to-succeed @ 00:23](https://youtu.be/zVY-NF6ZvEQ?t=23)
- At Google, being singularly focused on search (versus competitors trying to be portals doing everything else) was part of why they won [paul-buchheit-what-traits-do-startups-need-to-succeed @ 00:39](https://youtu.be/zVY-NF6ZvEQ?t=39)
