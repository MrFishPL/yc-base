# Fermat's Library Cofounders João Batalha and Luís Batalha — Compiled Truth

The Batalha brothers describe how their side-project annotation platform for academic papers, born from a personal journal club, is growing into a broader push for open science and open collaboration.

**Key ideas (with timestamps):**
- Fermat's Library is a platform for annotating academic papers, overlaying a PDF viewer with LaTeX/Markdown-capable annotations [fermat-s-library-cofounders-joa-o-batalha-and-lui-s-batalha @ 00:00](https://youtu.be/_iM9Ts_3qEk?t=0)
- The idea grew out of an informal journal club the four technical cofounders kept after college, where they'd read and present papers to each other [fermat-s-library-cofounders-joa-o-batalha-and-lui-s-batalha @ 00:42](https://youtu.be/_iM9Ts_3qEk?t=42)

- The larger goal behind Fermat's Library is to push science toward "open science" — open data, open code, and open publishing outside paywalled journals [fermat-s-library-cofounders-joa-o-batalha-and-lui-s-batalha @ 02:46](https://youtu.be/_iM9Ts_3qEk?t=166)
