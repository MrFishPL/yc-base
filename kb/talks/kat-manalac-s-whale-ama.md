# Kat Manalac's Whale AMA — Compiled Truth

Kat Manalac answers rapid-fire questions about the YC network, batch companies, and the single most important piece of advice for founders: talk to your users.

**Key ideas (with timestamps):**
- The YC network's biggest surprise for Kat was not how smart everyone is but how helpful alumni are, answering questions from any founder via the internal tool Bookface [kat-manalac-s-whale-ama @ 01:50](https://youtu.be/0eWRcwmjNBQ?t=110).
- If she could give YC founders only one piece of advice, it would be to talk to your users, since founders often over-polish product before getting it in front of real people [kat-manalac-s-whale-ama @ 02:46](https://youtu.be/0eWRcwmjNBQ?t=166).
- The best feedback comes from real, ideally paying, customers, not from more internal building and thinking [kat-manalac-s-whale-ama @ 03:05](https://youtu.be/0eWRcwmjNBQ?t=185).
- When reading applications, Kat runs a thought experiment imagining what the world looks like if hundreds of thousands or millions of people used the product, to evaluate its long-term vision [kat-manalac-s-whale-ama @ 03:22](https://youtu.be/0eWRcwmjNBQ?t=202).
- A bad co-founder relationship is infinitely worse than being a solo founder, and co-founder disputes are one of the biggest killers of promising early-stage companies [kat-manalac-s-whale-ama @ 05:35](https://youtu.be/0eWRcwmjNBQ?t=335).
- When evaluating applications, YC looks at how founders know each other and what they've built together before, whether class projects, past jobs, or side projects [kat-manalac-s-whale-ama @ 05:35](https://youtu.be/0eWRcwmjNBQ?t=335).
- All co-founding teams fight, but what matters is knowing how you will respond and react to each other during those fights [kat-manalac-s-whale-ama @ 05:58](https://youtu.be/0eWRcwmjNBQ?t=358).
- YC has funded strong Australian founders, including a company (proxy.com) making office key-card access simple via phone, and go1, a large marketplace for employee training and compliance video content [kat-manalac-s-whale-ama @ 04:03](https://youtu.be/0eWRcwmjNBQ?t=243).
