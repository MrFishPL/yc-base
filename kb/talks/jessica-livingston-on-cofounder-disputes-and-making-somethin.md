# Jessica Livingston on Cofounder Disputes and Making Something People Want — Compiled Truth

Jessica Livingston names founder breakups and failing to make something people want as the two biggest early startup killers, and argues both are addressed by choosing cofounders carefully and relentlessly talking to users to refine the idea.
