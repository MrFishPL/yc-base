# Selina Tobaccowala at the Female Founders Conference 2016 — Compiled Truth

The Evite co-founder and outgoing SurveyMonkey CTO/president shares what she learned scaling two companies, from aligning with co-founders up front to building culture, boards, and hiring practices that last.

**Key ideas (with timestamps):**
- Align with your co-founder up front on the important things — whether you're building to exit or build long-term, at what dollar amount you'd sell, and what company values and culture you want — rather than deciding in the heat of the moment [selina-tobaccowala-at-the-female-founders-conference-2016 @ 02:22](https://youtu.be/EHVzyc_Jl3Q?t=142)
- Evite raised $37 million for an online invitations business, which in hindsight was far more than needed — founders should think carefully upfront about how much capital they actually want to raise [selina-tobaccowala-at-the-female-founders-conference-2016 @ 03:13](https://youtu.be/EHVzyc_Jl3Q?t=193)
- Think about global and international users from the very beginning of building a product, since retrofitting language, currency, and design for international markets later is much harder [selina-tobaccowala-at-the-female-founders-conference-2016 @ 04:34](https://youtu.be/EHVzyc_Jl3Q?t=274)
- Build a culture of constant quantitative and qualitative testing ("always be testing") into both the product and the company from the start, since it compounds into major gains in conversion and engagement over time [selina-tobaccowala-at-the-female-founders-conference-2016 @ 05:11](https://youtu.be/EHVzyc_Jl3Q?t=311)
- Assemble a board early rather than too late, and make sure it includes independent members beyond just venture capitalists, since VCs who gain control can also fire you [selina-tobaccowala-at-the-female-founders-conference-2016 @ 07:56](https://youtu.be/EHVzyc_Jl3Q?t=476)
- As a company scales, leaders must deliberately keep engineers connected to why their work matters — SurveyMonkey embeds a business analyst on every team to read out the business impact of what was shipped [selina-tobaccowala-at-the-female-founders-conference-2016 @ 09:54](https://youtu.be/EHVzyc_Jl3Q?t=594)
- When evaluating any career or company decision, weigh three things: the people you'll work with, the product/mission, and what you'll personally learn [selina-tobaccowala-at-the-female-founders-conference-2016 @ 12:28](https://youtu.be/EHVzyc_Jl3Q?t=748)
- SurveyMonkey's hiring philosophy prioritized people who believed in the company's mission first, then favored promoting from within to give people the biggest opportunity of their career [selina-tobaccowala-at-the-female-founders-conference-2016 @ 16:18](https://youtu.be/EHVzyc_Jl3Q?t=978)
- Behavioral interview questions — asking how a candidate would react to someone treating another person poorly — help reveal culture fit beyond technical skill [selina-tobaccowala-at-the-female-founders-conference-2016 @ 22:00](https://youtu.be/EHVzyc_Jl3Q?t=1320)
- A trust-based, milestone-driven culture lets employees have real family flexibility, as long as goals are clearly communicated and consistently met [selina-tobaccowala-at-the-female-founders-conference-2016 @ 23:00](https://youtu.be/EHVzyc_Jl3Q?t=1380)
