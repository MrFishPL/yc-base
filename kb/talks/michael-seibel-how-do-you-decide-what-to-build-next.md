# Michael Seibel: How do you decide what to build next? — Compiled Truth

Michael Seibel argues that since most feature bets won't work, the goal is to build a fast, iterative process that quickly reveals what works rather than trying to be a genius who predicts the future.

**Key ideas (with timestamps):**
- Because there usually isn't a "right answer" and most things you want to build won't work, having a rapid development cycle lets you work on multiple bets at once [michael-seibel-how-do-you-decide-what-to-build-next @ 00:05](https://youtu.be/lI7wslGWIn0?t=5)
- You need a company process to build things quickly so you can see whether they actually work and iterate from there [michael-seibel-how-do-you-decide-what-to-build-next @ 00:25](https://youtu.be/lI7wslGWIn0?t=25)
- It's far more important to have a tactically talented team that can build MVPs quickly without frustration and measure results than to be a "super genius" who imagines the future without data [michael-seibel-how-do-you-decide-what-to-build-next @ 00:25](https://youtu.be/lI7wslGWIn0?t=25)
- You need big-picture imagination for a ten-year vision, but also tactical imagination for the next three months, and the latter is hard to nail without a fast-iteration process [michael-seibel-how-do-you-decide-what-to-build-next @ 00:46](https://youtu.be/lI7wslGWIn0?t=46)
- A process that can ship things quickly and then only iterate on what's working will serve a company far better than trying to perfect big bets upfront [michael-seibel-how-do-you-decide-what-to-build-next @ 01:05](https://youtu.be/lI7wslGWIn0?t=65)
- At justin.tv, their mistake was believing every feature had to be a "home run," aiming to swing for only four home runs, which meant each one took three months to try to make perfect [michael-seibel-how-do-you-decide-what-to-build-next @ 01:05](https://youtu.be/lI7wslGWIn0?t=65)
- That perfectionist, big-swing approach led into a "spiral of death" for the product [michael-seibel-how-do-you-decide-what-to-build-next @ 01:21](https://youtu.be/lI7wslGWIn0?t=81)
