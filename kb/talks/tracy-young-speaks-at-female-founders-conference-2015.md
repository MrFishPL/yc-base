# Tracy Young Speaks at Female Founders Conference 2015 — Compiled Truth

PlanGrid co-founder Tracy Young shares the founding story of a construction-blueprint startup, her co-founder Antoine's dying advice, and the lessons on patience, obsession, and courage that carried the company through hypergrowth.

**Key ideas (with timestamps):**
- PlanGrid was founded after Tracy Young experienced firsthand, as a construction engineer, how outdated paper blueprints caused costly on-site build errors [tracy-young-speaks-at-female-founders-conference-2015 @ 00:03](https://youtu.be/-pKR212H5vQ?t=3).
- As two non-technical domain experts, she and co-founder Ryan recruited a technical co-founder (her boyfriend Ralph, a Pixar engineer) after realizing they had a great problem but no way to build the product [tracy-young-speaks-at-female-founders-conference-2015 @ 05:49](https://youtu.be/-pKR212H5vQ?t=349).
- Co-founder Antoine's final advice before he passed from cancer was: life is short, take care of the people you love, don't be afraid to try new things, and never do anything that makes you unhappy [tracy-young-speaks-at-female-founders-conference-2015 @ 09:17](https://youtu.be/-pKR212H5vQ?t=557).
- The test for staying in a hard startup is asking "does this make me happy" and needing the answer to be yes every time, even in the lowest moments, or you'll compromise too much and burn out [tracy-young-speaks-at-female-founders-conference-2015 @ 12:45](https://youtu.be/-pKR212H5vQ?t=765).
- In 2011, when no field workers had iPads, the team maxed out their savings and credit cards to buy roughly 16 iPads themselves and hand them to customers rather than complain that no one had the hardware [tracy-young-speaks-at-female-founders-conference-2015 @ 17:25](https://youtu.be/-pKR212H5vQ?t=1045).
- PlanGrid's culture is built on obsessing over every detail — customer support, product polish, and even internal policy — because those choices form the foundation of the company [tracy-young-speaks-at-female-founders-conference-2015 @ 18:34](https://youtu.be/-pKR212H5vQ?t=1114).
- The company had to repeatedly tear down and rebuild its org structure (going flat with no titles up to ~60 people before adding management) and keeps reinventing departments as it scales [tracy-young-speaks-at-female-founders-conference-2015 @ 20:27](https://youtu.be/-pKR212H5vQ?t=1227).
- Larger, better-funded copycats could copy PlanGrid's UI but could not copy its functionality, ease of use, or customer support, so founders must stand their ground against bigger competitors [tracy-young-speaks-at-female-founders-conference-2015 @ 22:32](https://youtu.be/-pKR212H5vQ?t=1352).
- There is no shortcut for building usable software, fixing bugs, talking to users, and getting them to pay — only hard work and patience get you through [tracy-young-speaks-at-female-founders-conference-2015 @ 16:10](https://youtu.be/-pKR212H5vQ?t=970).
