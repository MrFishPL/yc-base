# How to Find a Cofounder - Kat Manalac — Compiled Truth

YC partner Kat Manalac lays out the data on where top YC founding teams met and what YC looks for in a co-founding team, arguing that while solo founding is possible, it's rare among the most successful companies.

**Key ideas (with timestamps):**
- 12% of the last YC batch were solo founders, but only 3 of the top 50 YC companies by valuation were started solo, showing solo founding is possible but hard [how-to-find-a-cofounder-kat-manalac @ 00:37](https://youtu.be/CRLzkbtKPGM?t=37)
- Having a co-founder gives you someone to split both tasks and the emotional burden of starting a company [how-to-find-a-cofounder-kat-manalac @ 00:57](https://youtu.be/CRLzkbtKPGM?t=57)
- Among YC companies valued over $100M, about half of the founding teams met in school (mostly during undergrad), 20% met at work, and 16% were introduced by mutual friends [how-to-find-a-cofounder-kat-manalac @ 00:57](https://youtu.be/CRLzkbtKPGM?t=57)
- Among female founders YC funded, about 33% met co-founders at school, 20% at work, and just under 10% at events [how-to-find-a-cofounder-kat-manalac @ 01:22](https://youtu.be/CRLzkbtKPGM?t=82)
- YC looks for well-balanced founding teams that can both build the product they're pitching and sell/communicate it clearly [how-to-find-a-cofounder-kat-manalac @ 02:04](https://youtu.be/CRLzkbtKPGM?t=124)
- YC likes to see teams that have already been working together for a while, as evidence they'll stick together for the long haul [how-to-find-a-cofounder-kat-manalac @ 02:23](https://youtu.be/CRLzkbtKPGM?t=143)
- Even if one co-founder joined months after the idea was conceived, YC still likes to see an even equity split given that time to IPO/exit averages 8-10 years [how-to-find-a-cofounder-kat-manalac @ 02:42](https://youtu.be/CRLzkbtKPGM?t=162)
- If a potential co-founder offers you a spot on their team without equal equity, it may signal they won't truly value your contributions [how-to-find-a-cofounder-kat-manalac @ 03:19](https://youtu.be/CRLzkbtKPGM?t=199)
- 94% of top YC companies have co-founders who typically met at school or work, and favor an equal equity split [how-to-find-a-cofounder-kat-manalac @ 03:19](https://youtu.be/CRLzkbtKPGM?t=199)
