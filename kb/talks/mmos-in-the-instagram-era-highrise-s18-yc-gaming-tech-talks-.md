# MMOs in the Instagram Era: Highrise (S18) - YC Gaming Tech Talks 2020 — Compiled Truth

Pocket Worlds' Highrise is a "social first" mobile MMO built with a hybrid native-app/game-engine architecture designed to keep players' social lives inside the app instead of losing them to Instagram or Snapchat after they finish playing.

**Key ideas (with timestamps):**
- Highrise has over 5 million downloads and grosses over a million dollars a month in revenue [mmos-in-the-instagram-era-highrise-s18-yc-gaming-tech-talks- @ 00:02](https://youtu.be/ztoZF65LUgk?t=2)
- Under the "social first" philosophy, the team built the messenger, news feed, guilds, profiles, and economy first, and only then added the actual mini-game (a fashion contest) [mmos-in-the-instagram-era-highrise-s18-yc-gaming-tech-talks- @ 01:10](https://youtu.be/ztoZF65LUgk?t=70)
- Social-first design solves the long-term attention problem that plagues most mobile games, since players join for the game but stay for the friends they make [mmos-in-the-instagram-era-highrise-s18-yc-gaming-tech-talks- @ 01:55](https://youtu.be/ztoZF65LUgk?t=115)
- Without a genuinely app-like social experience, users would play the game, make friends, then move those relationships to Snapchat or Instagram once done — which is what happened in Highrise's early days [mmos-in-the-instagram-era-highrise-s18-yc-gaming-tech-talks- @ 02:13](https://youtu.be/ztoZF65LUgk?t=133)
- The team concluded that no game engine like Unity could match native iOS/Android UI quality, so they built an architecture that is 50% Swift/Kotlin for UI and 50% shared C++ for the game engine [mmos-in-the-instagram-era-highrise-s18-yc-gaming-tech-talks- @ 03:00](https://youtu.be/ztoZF65LUgk?t=180)
- The native UI and C++ game core are connected via feature-separated bridges (Objective-C++ on iOS, JNI on Android), creating a swappable microservices-like architecture [mmos-in-the-instagram-era-highrise-s18-yc-gaming-tech-talks- @ 03:24](https://youtu.be/ztoZF65LUgk?t=204)
- This architecture lets Pocket Worlds swap out the mini-game and meta-game (e.g. a hero collector or fishing game) while reusing the same avatar, world, messenger, and guild systems [mmos-in-the-instagram-era-highrise-s18-yc-gaming-tech-talks- @ 03:49](https://youtu.be/ztoZF65LUgk?t=229)
- Pocket Worlds has been fully remote for four years and had just closed a Series A to build more games and worlds [mmos-in-the-instagram-era-highrise-s18-yc-gaming-tech-talks- @ 04:56](https://youtu.be/ztoZF65LUgk?t=296)
