# How to Survive the Crypto Boom & Bust Cycle — Compiled Truth

CoinTracker co-founder Chandan Lodha describes how building in crypto means surviving brutal bear-market winters by staying true to first principles, being radically honest with the team, and refocusing obsessively on users and product to be ready for the next bull run.

**Key ideas (with timestamps):**
- CoinTracker was born out of the founders' own frustration with slow, inefficient traditional financial rails while building an unrelated fintech product in 2017, which pushed them into crypto portfolio tracking [how-to-survive-the-crypto-boom-bust-cycle @ 01:23](https://youtu.be/8-Zrhz1QKGo?t=83)
- Crypto's boom-bust cycle is unusually extreme compared to other tech spaces: huge surges of growth and demand concentrated in bull runs followed by year-plus bear periods where the space is "declared dead" [how-to-survive-the-crypto-boom-bust-cycle @ 02:47](https://youtu.be/8-Zrhz1QKGo?t=167)
- In 2020 CoinTracker was six months from shutting down, and Lodha calls that crypto winter the lowest point of his professional life, describing a feeling of desperation compounded by the whole world calling the space irrelevant [how-to-survive-the-crypto-boom-bust-cycle @ 03:28](https://youtu.be/8-Zrhz1QKGo?t=208)
- They survived by taking a long-term, first-principles view of whether the mission still made sense regardless of the current climate, and by being radically open and honest with the team about the harsh reality and what needed to change [how-to-survive-the-crypto-boom-bust-cycle @ 04:08](https://youtu.be/8-Zrhz1QKGo?t=248)
- During the 2021 DeFi Summer bull run CoinTracker grew 14x by focusing heavily on user growth, but as the market cooled, user feedback revealed they had lost their product edge and hadn't kept pace with new DeFi activity like liquidity pools and Uniswap [how-to-survive-the-crypto-boom-bust-cycle @ 05:24](https://youtu.be/8-Zrhz1QKGo?t=324)
- Facing that painful feedback, CoinTracker halted other work for nine months to rebuild the product around what customers actually needed [how-to-survive-the-crypto-boom-bust-cycle @ 06:03](https://youtu.be/8-Zrhz1QKGo?t=363)
- The rebuild rested on two changes: elevating "user obsession" so everyone from support to engineering constantly talks to users, and shifting the guiding principle from "think long-term" to "think long-term, ship today," which gave the team permission to take risks and accelerated execution almost overnight [how-to-survive-the-crypto-boom-bust-cycle @ 06:22](https://youtu.be/8-Zrhz1QKGo?t=382)
- Chandan frames the lesson as reinforcing YC's core advice: no matter how much a company grows, it can't stop building product and talking to users, especially in a fast-changing space like crypto [how-to-survive-the-crypto-boom-bust-cycle @ 07:45](https://youtu.be/8-Zrhz1QKGo?t=465)
- His overall advice for surviving crypto cycles is to take a long-term view, stay humble during bull runs, and keep believing during bear markets, since it's the companies that survive the winters that reap the rewards of the next boom [how-to-survive-the-crypto-boom-bust-cycle @ 08:22](https://youtu.be/8-Zrhz1QKGo?t=502)
