# Helping Landlords Find Tenants – Sean Mitchell of Rezi — Compiled Truth

Rezi co-founder Sean Mitchell recounts pivoting during YC from a tenant-screening SaaS tool to a rental marketplace that guarantees landlords a lease and eliminates vacancy risk, and the operational, financing, and personal lessons that followed.

**Key ideas (with timestamps):**
- Rezi pivoted mid-YC from selling landlords better tenant-screening software to a bolder offer — guaranteeing landlords they'll never have a vacant unit — deciding the strategy within days of getting in and closing the first transaction before the month was out [helping-landlords-find-tenants-sean-mitchell-of-rezi @ 02:20](https://youtu.be/q0dzmQ1HvGY?t=140)
- The founders risked roughly 40% of the company's cash on the very first transaction to prove the new model worked [helping-landlords-find-tenants-sean-mitchell-of-rezi @ 05:19](https://youtu.be/q0dzmQ1HvGY?t=319)
- To build the landlord supply side, two co-founders ran a manual cold-calling operation, pulling units off Craigslist/Zillow and calling roughly 1,000 landlords, with about 90% rejecting them, to learn what landlords actually needed [helping-landlords-find-tenants-sean-mitchell-of-rezi @ 10:37](https://youtu.be/q0dzmQ1HvGY?t=637)
- Tenant approval is deliberately automated and algorithmic, screening only on rental history, credit, and income so no landlord bias enters the decision [helping-landlords-find-tenants-sean-mitchell-of-rezi @ 13:54](https://youtu.be/q0dzmQ1HvGY?t=834)
- Rather than spend VC equity to fund apartment-leasing originations, Rezi built a separate debt/securitization structure so investor equity funds operations while a "financing mousetrap" of receivables funds the leasing product [helping-landlords-find-tenants-sean-mitchell-of-rezi @ 17:30](https://youtu.be/q0dzmQ1HvGY?t=1050)
- Studying other successful startups, he was surprised the biggest "aha" was that many just ran normal sales teams rather than fancy innovative acquisition channels — sometimes the standard process is standard because it's the right one [helping-landlords-find-tenants-sean-mitchell-of-rezi @ 31:09](https://youtu.be/q0dzmQ1HvGY?t=1869)
- His main advice to his past self is to stay hyper-focused on solving the actual problem rather than running a startup "for startup's sake," and to be willing to kill features the market doesn't want [helping-landlords-find-tenants-sean-mitchell-of-rezi @ 41:37](https://youtu.be/q0dzmQ1HvGY?t=2497)
- His recruiting rule is to hire precisely for what the role needs right now rather than an idealized future senior hire, since an early startup only has runway for six months to a year and a half [helping-landlords-find-tenants-sean-mitchell-of-rezi @ 45:29](https://youtu.be/q0dzmQ1HvGY?t=2729)
- To manage founder anxiety he learned to stop, physically step away, and journal why he's feeling a certain way, which helps the logical brain take over from raw emotion [helping-landlords-find-tenants-sean-mitchell-of-rezi @ 54:52](https://youtu.be/q0dzmQ1HvGY?t=3292)
