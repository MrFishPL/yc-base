# Work at a Startup Expo 2019 — Compiled Truth

At YC's Work at a Startup Expo, YC's Ryan and incoming president Geoff Ralston make the case for joining a startup over "the man," followed by dozens of two-minute founder pitches for engineering hires.

- Startups build self-reliance, resilience, and communication skills, and give you the closest bond you'll ever have with a team [work-at-a-startup-expo-2019 @ 25:29](https://youtu.be/7bGZAj7G4WI?t=1529)
