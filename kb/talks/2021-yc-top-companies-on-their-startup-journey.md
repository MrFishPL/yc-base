# 2021 YC Top Companies on Their Startup Journey — Compiled Truth

Founders from several YC top companies (including Nova Credit) share what it actually took to build their companies: authentic motivation, relentless focus on one or two metrics, and deliberate culture-building alongside growth.

**Key ideas (with timestamps):**
- No matter how successful a company looks from the outside, the journey is longer and harder than founders imagine, and building on an inauthentic idea makes it far easier to give up during the hard days [2021-yc-top-companies-on-their-startup-journey @ 00:20](https://youtu.be/QBOxjlN9M2E?t=20)
- Founders should start with introspection about why they personally want to solve this problem, in this market, and whether that motivation feels authentic [2021-yc-top-companies-on-their-startup-journey @ 00:56](https://youtu.be/QBOxjlN9M2E?t=56)
- Building something meaningful requires thinking in units of a decade, since it demands round-the-clock commitment over years [2021-yc-top-companies-on-their-startup-journey @ 01:16](https://youtu.be/QBOxjlN9M2E?t=76)
- Because competitors typically have more money, people, and resources, persistence driven by genuinely wanting to see the change in the world is what carries great companies through [2021-yc-top-companies-on-their-startup-journey @ 01:52](https://youtu.be/QBOxjlN9M2E?t=112)
- Early on it's easy to get distracted chasing trends (like bolting on AI or a chatbot to get press) instead of staying on what actually matters for the business [2021-yc-top-companies-on-their-startup-journey @ 02:10](https://youtu.be/QBOxjlN9M2E?t=130)
- The single biggest lesson was the power of extreme focus: pick one or two metrics that matter and track weekly growth on them relentlessly [2021-yc-top-companies-on-their-startup-journey @ 02:30](https://youtu.be/QBOxjlN9M2E?t=150)
- Breaking the company into phases with a defined milestone for each phase creates a system of incremental, trackable progress [2021-yc-top-companies-on-their-startup-journey @ 03:06](https://youtu.be/QBOxjlN9M2E?t=186)
- During the YC batch, having the entire team focus on nothing but growing the numbers for three months was extremely valuable and shaped how the company operates afterward [2021-yc-top-companies-on-their-startup-journey @ 03:25](https://youtu.be/QBOxjlN9M2E?t=205)
- Founders often focus on product and product-market fit while neglecting to define company values and build the right culture from day one [2021-yc-top-companies-on-their-startup-journey @ 03:47](https://youtu.be/QBOxjlN9M2E?t=227)
- One team highlights building a deliberately diverse organization (50% women, 40% non-white, 15% Black and Latinx) as core to hiring the best people and setting them up for success [2021-yc-top-companies-on-their-startup-journey @ 04:09](https://youtu.be/QBOxjlN9M2E?t=249)
