# Zynga Founder: Consumer Is Not Investible Right Now - Thats Why You Should Build It — Compiled Truth

Zynga founder Mark Pincus argues that even though investors currently shun consumer startups, AI and agents make this the best moment in years to reinvent old internet services, and he shares his "proven, better, new" product framework and views on founder mode.
