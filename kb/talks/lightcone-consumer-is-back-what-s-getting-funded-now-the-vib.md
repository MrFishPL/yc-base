# Lightcone: Consumer is back, What's getting funded now, The vibes immaculate — Compiled Truth

The Winter 2024 YC batch marked a platform shift toward AI, developer tools, and consumer ideas, away from international/local-market clones, fintech copies, and crypto, with founders shipping real, monetizing products faster than ever.

**Key ideas (with timestamps):**
- Just under 70% of the Winter 2024 batch (about 170 companies) were AI ideas, versus only 8% in Winter 2020 [lightcone-consumer-is-back-what-s-getting-funded-now-the-vib @ 02:00](https://youtu.be/e1Yhs9BEOSw?t=120)
- Consumer startup ideas are visibly returning after years of founders defaulting to B2B SaaS, partly because the fear of "Facebook will just clone and crush you" has faded [lightcone-consumer-is-back-what-s-getting-funded-now-the-vib @ 04:21](https://youtu.be/e1Yhs9BEOSw?t=261)
- Dev tools grew about 30% versus four years earlier, driven by technical founders building the "hammer" infrastructure (RAG, indexing, evals) needed before AI apps can be built at scale [lightcone-consumer-is-back-what-s-getting-funded-now-the-vib @ 07:00](https://youtu.be/e1Yhs9BEOSw?t=420)
- Open-source dev tool companies jumped from 5 in Winter 2020 to 22 in this batch, following a repeating pattern (Slack/Mattermost, GitHub/GitLab) where open-source challengers can unseat closed dominant platforms [lightcone-consumer-is-back-what-s-getting-funded-now-the-vib @ 12:34](https://youtu.be/e1Yhs9BEOSw?t=754)
- This is the most technical batch ever, with 99% having a technical founder versus 88% during the pandemic, reflecting a swing away from the "tech-enabled" (WeWork-style) founder profile back toward core software/AI builders [lightcone-consumer-is-back-what-s-getting-funded-now-the-vib @ 13:38](https://youtu.be/e1Yhs9BEOSw?t=818)
- International applications dropped from 45% (Winter 2020) to 20% now, and marketplace ideas fell 4x, because the "copy a US model to another country" opportunity has largely run its course [lightcone-consumer-is-back-what-s-getting-funded-now-the-vib @ 22:09](https://youtu.be/e1Yhs9BEOSw?t=1329)
- Crypto applications have not surged despite a fresh Bitcoin bull run, because mindshare among top engineers has shifted almost entirely to AI, and past regulatory enforcement left a chilling, traumatic effect on crypto founders [lightcone-consumer-is-back-what-s-getting-funded-now-the-vib @ 26:10](https://youtu.be/e1Yhs9BEOSw?t=1570)
- Over 80% of the batch had no revenue and hadn't launched at application time, yet the batch's combined ARR tripled from $6M in January to $20M by demo day [lightcone-consumer-is-back-what-s-getting-funded-now-the-vib @ 37:20](https://youtu.be/e1Yhs9BEOSw?t=2240)
