# Common Mistakes With Vibe Coded Websites — Compiled Truth

A YC design review of AI-coded startup landing pages showing how AI design tools make professional-looking sites trivially easy to build, while also creating a wave of ubiquitous, non-original visual clichés that founders should consciously edit rather than accept by default.

**Key ideas (with timestamps):**
- AI design tools make a professional-looking site achievable for anyone, but overused effects (purple gradients, fade-ins) become so ubiquitous they lose all meaning and originality [common-mistakes-with-vibe-coded-websites @ 01:56](https://youtu.be/DNSXlBmukck?t=116)
- Because LLMs train on whatever good websites get linked to, a single trend (like a purple gradient) can spread to nearly every startup site within a week [common-mistakes-with-vibe-coded-websites @ 02:16](https://youtu.be/DNSXlBmukck?t=136)
- Distracting scroll-following elements (like a line that tracks the cursor) are things AI makes easy to build but that add no value and can distract from conversion [common-mistakes-with-vibe-coded-websites @ 03:47](https://youtu.be/DNSXlBmukck?t=227)
- Well-executed hover animations (like game-controller card effects) can reinforce brand identity in ways that would have been too expensive to build pre-AI [common-mistakes-with-vibe-coded-websites @ 04:46](https://youtu.be/DNSXlBmukck?t=286)
- Hover effects should make elements feel more clickable, not fade them away — the browser's default hand cursor already signals interactivity [common-mistakes-with-vibe-coded-websites @ 06:15](https://youtu.be/DNSXlBmukck?t=375)
- Hover shouldn't be used to hide essential functionality or information since there's no equivalent of hover on mobile [common-mistakes-with-vibe-coded-websites @ 07:20](https://youtu.be/DNSXlBmukck?t=440)
- Heavy scroll-jacking and scroll-triggered animation sequences make sites feel slow ("molasses") and disorient users about how far they are through the page [common-mistakes-with-vibe-coded-websites @ 18:45](https://youtu.be/DNSXlBmukck?t=1125)
- Fake dashboards with primary-color icon callouts and generic bento-box layouts are hallmark tells of AI-generated design that reduce a company's originality [common-mistakes-with-vibe-coded-websites @ 27:21](https://youtu.be/DNSXlBmukck?t=1641)
- Founders should use AI to save time on execution, then reinvest that saved time into sharpening messaging and product thinking rather than accepting default AI output [common-mistakes-with-vibe-coded-websites @ 30:11](https://youtu.be/DNSXlBmukck?t=1811)
- A landing page is a customer acquisition channel, not the product itself, so founders must stay intentional about brand and originality rather than outsourcing design thinking to LLMs [common-mistakes-with-vibe-coded-websites @ 34:48](https://youtu.be/DNSXlBmukck?t=2088)
