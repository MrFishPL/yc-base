# Startup Advisor Equity? - Pebble Watch Founder Eric Migicovsky — Compiled Truth

Eric Migicovsky explains how he built and compensated a network of startup advisors, favoring people a few years ahead of him in the same domain, and describes typical advisor equity grants.

- He found the most success with advisors who were about three to five years ahead of him in the same domain, in his case consumer electronics [startup-advisor-equity-pebble-watch-founder-eric-migicovsky @ 00:19](https://youtu.be/XvlKwwKfS5c?t=19)
