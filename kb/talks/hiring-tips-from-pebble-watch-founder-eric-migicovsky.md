# Hiring Tips from Pebble Watch Founder Eric Migicovsky — Compiled Truth

Eric Migicovsky argues that what you should look for in early hires changes with company stage, and at the earliest stage the priority is flexibility, trust, and multidisciplinary skill rather than deep expertise.

**Key ideas (with timestamps):**
- What to look for in a recruit depends heavily on the stage of the company [hiring-tips-from-pebble-watch-founder-eric-migicovsky @ 00:18](https://youtu.be/IGj7CK-K6q4?t=18)
- At the earliest stage, the top skill to hire for is flexibility of ideas, since there is no fixed roadmap and the product will inevitably change [hiring-tips-from-pebble-watch-founder-eric-migicovsky @ 00:41](https://youtu.be/IGj7CK-K6q4?t=41)
- Early hires should attack the startup like a problem-solving mission rather than just clocking in and out [hiring-tips-from-pebble-watch-founder-eric-migicovsky @ 01:05](https://youtu.be/IGj7CK-K6q4?t=65)
- Trust is one of the most important qualities at every stage, learned the hard way after bad experiences hiring untrusted contractors [hiring-tips-from-pebble-watch-founder-eric-migicovsky @ 01:05](https://youtu.be/IGj7CK-K6q4?t=65)
- The best hires were friends and former classmates he had already built personal trust and working history with [hiring-tips-from-pebble-watch-founder-eric-migicovsky @ 01:31](https://youtu.be/IGj7CK-K6q4?t=91)
- Trusting a hire to solve a problem independently means you don't have to constantly worry whether or how it will get solved [hiring-tips-from-pebble-watch-founder-eric-migicovsky @ 01:58](https://youtu.be/IGj7CK-K6q4?t=118)
- Without trust in your hires, you end up having to do the work yourself [hiring-tips-from-pebble-watch-founder-eric-migicovsky @ 02:20](https://youtu.be/IGj7CK-K6q4?t=140)
- A multidisciplinary skill set matters early on because founders and early employees must design, debug, talk to customers, and work with partners on a daily basis [hiring-tips-from-pebble-watch-founder-eric-migicovsky @ 02:37](https://youtu.be/IGj7CK-K6q4?t=157)
- He would not optimize for hiring deep domain experts early on, since a narrow skillset can penalize the team's ability to pivot direction later [hiring-tips-from-pebble-watch-founder-eric-migicovsky @ 03:29](https://youtu.be/IGj7CK-K6q4?t=209)
- His top three qualities for an early hire are trust, flexibility of creativity, and a multidisciplinary skill set [hiring-tips-from-pebble-watch-founder-eric-migicovsky @ 04:00](https://youtu.be/IGj7CK-K6q4?t=240)
