# Apple Vision Pro: Startup Platform Of The Future? — Compiled Truth

The YC Lightcone hosts, joined by AR/VR veteran Diana Hu, unpack whether the Apple Vision Pro is a genuine new startup platform (an "iPhone moment") or a niche "Newton moment," and what that means for founders deciding whether to build for it now.

**Key ideas (with timestamps):**
- Apple's pass-through video approach to the Vision Pro sidesteps the harder optical-AR physics problem that stalled HoloLens and Magic Leap, trading it for a still-hard but more tractable rendering challenge [apple-vision-pro-startup-platform-of-the-future @ 04:13](https://youtu.be/a_n4yOqlWbA?t=253)
- The device relies heavily on eye tracking for foveated rendering, and Diana Hu argues this is exactly the kind of new UX primitive founders should watch, similar to how capacitive touch unlocked the iPhone [apple-vision-pro-startup-platform-of-the-future @ 13:34](https://youtu.be/a_n4yOqlWbA?t=814)
- Just as third-party developers (not Apple) invented interactions like pull-to-refresh after the iPhone shipped, new Vision Pro interaction patterns will likely be discovered by outside developers, not Apple itself [apple-vision-pro-startup-platform-of-the-future @ 14:48](https://youtu.be/a_n4yOqlWbA?t=888)
- It took about five years after the iPhone launched for defining mobile companies like Instacart, DoorDash, and Uber to emerge, because that's how long it took for 70-80% of society to have always-on smartphones [apple-vision-pro-startup-platform-of-the-future @ 19:02](https://youtu.be/a_n4yOqlWbA?t=1142)
- Mobile didn't just enable new apps, it turned previously bad ideas (like Webvan-style grocery delivery) into good ones, as happened with Instacart [apple-vision-pro-startup-platform-of-the-future @ 22:18](https://youtu.be/a_n4yOqlWbA?t=1338)
- A risk for the Vision Pro is the "Tesla Roadster" failure mode: a great high-end halo product that never gets a mass-market follow-up like the Model 3, which would stall the developer ecosystem needed to make it relevant [apple-vision-pro-startup-platform-of-the-future @ 20:49](https://youtu.be/a_n4yOqlWbA?t=1249)
- YC's approach to platform shifts is to avoid a strong top-down thesis and instead evaluate each founder's specific application idea from first principles [apple-vision-pro-startup-platform-of-the-future @ 25:37](https://youtu.be/a_n4yOqlWbA?t=1537)
- The clearest early tell of a good Vision Pro founder is irrational, pre-existing personal conviction, e.g. someone who was already building VR apps in their free time before it was popular [apple-vision-pro-startup-platform-of-the-future @ 27:07](https://youtu.be/a_n4yOqlWbA?t=1627)
