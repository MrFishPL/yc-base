# Fei-Fei Li: Spatial Intelligence is the Next Frontier in AI — Compiled Truth

Fei-Fei Li argues that spatial intelligence — understanding, generating, and reasoning about the 3D world — is the next great unsolved frontier in AI, harder than language because the world is 3D, multi-sensory, and not purely generative, and she's now building it at World Labs.

**Key ideas (with timestamps):**
- ImageNet was conceived around 2007 out of a bet that a paradigm shift to data-driven methods, not just algorithms, was needed to solve generalization in computer vision [fei-fei-li-spatial-intelligence-is-the-next-frontier-in-ai @ 03:35](https://youtu.be/_PioN-CpOP0?t=215)
- The 2012 "AlexNet moment" combined convolutional neural networks with the first use of two GPUs together for deep learning, marking the convergence of data, compute, and algorithms [fei-fei-li-spatial-intelligence-is-the-next-frontier-in-ai @ 07:54](https://youtu.be/_PioN-CpOP0?t=474)
- She sees spatial intelligence as harder than language because the world is 3D (4D with time), vision is a mathematically ill-posed projection from 3D to 2D, and the world is not purely generative but must also be reconstructed and obey physics [fei-fei-li-spatial-intelligence-is-the-next-frontier-in-ai @ 19:36](https://youtu.be/_PioN-CpOP0?t=1176)
- AGI will not be complete without spatial intelligence, which is why she founded World Labs with Justin Johnson, Ben Mildenhall, and Christoph Lassner to build world models that go beyond flat pixels and language [fei-fei-li-spatial-intelligence-is-the-next-frontier-in-ai @ 16:56](https://youtu.be/_PioN-CpOP0?t=1016)
- Unlike language, there is no readily available internet-scale dataset for spatial intelligence, so World Labs is taking a hybrid approach to data while emphasizing that quality matters as much as quantity ("garbage in garbage out") [fei-fei-li-spatial-intelligence-is-the-next-frontier-in-ai @ 41:34](https://youtu.be/_PioN-CpOP0?t=2494)
- Her core hiring criterion, both for advising students and hiring at World Labs, is "intellectual fearlessness" — the courage to embrace hard problems and go all in [fei-fei-li-spatial-intelligence-is-the-next-frontier-in-ai @ 30:24](https://youtu.be/_PioN-CpOP0?t=1824)
- She deliberately seeks out the hardest, "bordering delusional" problems, reasoning that if a problem were easy someone else would have already solved it [fei-fei-li-spatial-intelligence-is-the-next-frontier-in-ai @ 21:24](https://youtu.be/_PioN-CpOP0?t=1284)
- Before founding World Labs she ran a laundromat at 19 to support her family, calling herself the founder, CEO, and cashier of that business, which shaped her entrepreneurial resilience [fei-fei-li-spatial-intelligence-is-the-next-frontier-in-ai @ 26:23](https://youtu.be/_PioN-CpOP0?t=1583)
- Her advice for founders and students facing moments of doubt is to "forget about what you have done in the past, forget about what others think of you, just hunker down and build" [fei-fei-li-spatial-intelligence-is-the-next-frontier-in-ai @ 28:50](https://youtu.be/_PioN-CpOP0?t=1730)
- She believes graduate school should be led by pure curiosity, whereas a startup cannot be driven by curiosity alone because investors demand a focused commercial goal [fei-fei-li-spatial-intelligence-is-the-next-frontier-in-ai @ 37:56](https://youtu.be/_PioN-CpOP0?t=2276)
