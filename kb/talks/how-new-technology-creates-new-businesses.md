# How New Technology Creates New Businesses — Compiled Truth

Dalton and Michael argue that every major technology shift (the internet, eBay, cloud compute, smartphones) has triggered a wave of new founders who spot the opportunity early, and that AI is the next such wave.

**Key ideas (with timestamps):**
- Each new wave of tools historically triggers an explosion of new founders who can suddenly create value, as seen when people who learned to build websites in the early internet era made large amounts of money with basic skills [how-new-technology-creates-new-businesses @ 00:44](https://youtu.be/KxjPgGLVJSg?t=44)
- Being among the first to understand a brand-new technology means you're not competing with anyone yet — it's greenfield opportunity [how-new-technology-creates-new-businesses @ 02:44](https://youtu.be/KxjPgGLVJSg?t=164)
- A major technology shift typically reduces the cost of starting some category of business by up to 10x, making businesses viable that previously wouldn't have made sense for an ordinary person to attempt [how-new-technology-creates-new-businesses @ 03:28](https://youtu.be/KxjPgGLVJSg?t=208)

- These technology-driven opportunities span the entire spectrum, from businesses that could become venture-backed billion-dollar companies to ones that simply let someone pay rent and live well [how-new-technology-creates-new-businesses @ 05:34](https://youtu.be/KxjPgGLVJSg?t=334)
