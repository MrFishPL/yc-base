# Geoff Ralston - Parting Advice — Compiled Truth

Closing out Startup School, Geoff Ralston argues every founder's path is unique, that things will always go wrong, and that the antidote to adversity is default-to-action combined with genuine benevolence toward co-founders, employees, customers, and investors.

**Key ideas (with timestamps):**
- Every startup and every founder has a different path, so don't be distracted by comparing yourself to what other startups are doing [geoff-ralston-parting-advice @ 00:57](https://youtu.be/s8ER6DIlAvY?t=57)
- Focus on what you learned at Startup School and, above all, on making something people want [geoff-ralston-parting-advice @ 03:43](https://youtu.be/s8ER6DIlAvY?t=223)
- Things will always go wrong at a startup — that's almost the beauty of it, and founders are the "problem solvers in chief" [geoff-ralston-parting-advice @ 04:35](https://youtu.be/s8ER6DIlAvY?t=275)
