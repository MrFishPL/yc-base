# How Much Should You Spend After Fundraising? - Gustaf Alströmer — Compiled Truth

Gustaf Alströmer warns that too many startups spend seed money too fast, and argues founders should budget as if they'll never raise another round, using clear milestones and runway discipline instead.

**Key ideas (with timestamps):**
- Too many companies spend way too much of their raised money too fast, and running out of money is a top reason companies die [how-much-should-you-spend-after-fundraising-gustaf-alstr-mer @ 00:00](https://youtu.be/6IPuXAK24RE?t=0)
- You should spend money assuming you'll probably never be able to raise another round, since most seed-stage companies never raise a Series A, and most Series A companies never raise a Series B [how-much-should-you-spend-after-fundraising-gustaf-alstr-mer @ 00:16](https://youtu.be/6IPuXAK24RE?t=16)
- A founder's goal is to make the company survive, not to raise another round; raising is a means to an end, and doing it means owning less of your company [how-much-should-you-spend-after-fundraising-gustaf-alstr-mer @ 00:36](https://youtu.be/6IPuXAK24RE?t=36)
- Set a clear set of milestones with good metrics for what you plan to achieve with your fundraise, typically over about 24 months [how-much-should-you-spend-after-fundraising-gustaf-alstr-mer @ 00:53](https://youtu.be/6IPuXAK24RE?t=53)
- Plan to start raising your next round when you have about 8 months of runway left, leaving 16 months to hit your milestones [how-much-should-you-spend-after-fundraising-gustaf-alstr-mer @ 01:12](https://youtu.be/6IPuXAK24RE?t=72)
- Once your company starts generating revenue, you can increase spend on things like hiring and marketing, but not faster than the revenue you're bringing in [how-much-should-you-spend-after-fundraising-gustaf-alstr-mer @ 01:12](https://youtu.be/6IPuXAK24RE?t=72)
- A trick YC sometimes advises: put half of your seed round in a separate bank account and mentally treat it as unavailable for the first year, which forces more frugal spending [how-much-should-you-spend-after-fundraising-gustaf-alstr-mer @ 01:31](https://youtu.be/6IPuXAK24RE?t=91)
- Because raising another round is so hard, you should always spend your money as if you'll never be able to raise again [how-much-should-you-spend-after-fundraising-gustaf-alstr-mer @ 01:49](https://youtu.be/6IPuXAK24RE?t=109)
