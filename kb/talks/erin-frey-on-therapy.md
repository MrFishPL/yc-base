# Erin Frey on Therapy — Compiled Truth

Erin Frey, co-founder and CEO of Kip, describes how stress and anxiety degraded her decision-making and confidence as a founder, and how starting therapy through her own company's product helped her recover and perform better.

**Key ideas (with timestamps):**
- Unmanaged stress and anxiety made Erin think less clearly, make slower and worse decisions, and fight more with her co-founder [erin-frey-on-therapy @ 00:23](https://youtu.be/LEgbK735lfw?t=23)
- As a founder, every day matters, and a bad week or several bad weeks in a row compound into serious problems [erin-frey-on-therapy @ 01:01](https://youtu.be/LEgbK735lfw?t=61)
- Therapy starts with working with a therapist to identify a goal, even if you don't yet know what's really wrong (she thought it was confidence; it was actually stress and anxiety) [erin-frey-on-therapy @ 01:56](https://youtu.be/LEgbK735lfw?t=116)
- Over ongoing sessions you learn your thought patterns and which behaviors help or hurt you, building tools to navigate emotions better [erin-frey-on-therapy @ 02:17](https://youtu.be/LEgbK735lfw?t=137)
- A concrete founder technique from therapy is examining catastrophic thoughts before a pitch by asking whether there's real evidence for them, and identifying that the worst case is rarely as bad as it feels [erin-frey-on-therapy @ 02:53](https://youtu.be/LEgbK735lfw?t=173)
- Therapy gave her a safe space to discuss problems she couldn't share with her co-founder, family, or friends [erin-frey-on-therapy @ 04:05](https://youtu.be/LEgbK735lfw?t=245)
- Once anxiety and stress reduced, she started viewing problems as exciting challenges again instead of behemoths destined to fail [erin-frey-on-therapy @ 04:45](https://youtu.be/LEgbK735lfw?t=285)
- Stress, anxiety, and depression are predictable patterns among founders given the uncertainty and pressure of running a startup, so getting help early matters [erin-frey-on-therapy @ 05:44](https://youtu.be/LEgbK735lfw?t=344)
- She argues founders should treat mental health maintenance like routine dental or physical checkups rather than waiting for a crisis, since the brain is a founder's most important asset [erin-frey-on-therapy @ 06:10](https://youtu.be/LEgbK735lfw?t=370)
