# Why Fundraising Is Different In Silicon Valley - Michael Seibel — Compiled Truth

Michael Seibel explains why founders who fail to raise money in their local, less active startup communities shouldn't conclude their idea is bad, because Bay Area investors evaluate ideas very differently than investors elsewhere.

**Key ideas (with timestamps):**
- A founder from North Carolina struggled to raise locally and worried they'd never be able to raise in Silicon Valley [why-fundraising-is-different-in-silicon-valley-michael-seibe @ 00:00](https://youtu.be/oAEZoAp2_zY?t=0)
- Investors are not spread evenly around the world, and Bay Area investors think about deals differently than investors elsewhere [why-fundraising-is-different-in-silicon-valley-michael-seibe @ 00:20](https://youtu.be/oAEZoAp2_zY?t=20)
- Long-tenured Bay Area early-stage investors have all said no to companies that later became huge successes, which makes them pay extra attention and dig deeper before passing on a new company [why-fundraising-is-different-in-silicon-valley-michael-seibe @ 00:47](https://youtu.be/oAEZoAp2_zY?t=47)
- In many other parts of the country or world, angel investors see fewer deals and fewer good deals, so nearly every time they've said no they've actually been right [why-fundraising-is-different-in-silicon-valley-michael-seibe @ 01:08](https://youtu.be/oAEZoAp2_zY?t=68)
- Because of that dichotomy, Bay Area investors are much less likely to dismiss an idea out of hand and are more likely to dig into execution, monetization, and scale potential, whereas investors outside strong startup communities are more likely to just judge whether the idea itself sounds good [why-fundraising-is-different-in-silicon-valley-michael-seibe @ 01:31](https://youtu.be/oAEZoAp2_zY?t=91)
- Having reviewed hundreds of thousands of YC applications, Seibel says his ability to judge whether an idea is good is much weaker than his ability to judge whether a team is executing well [why-fundraising-is-different-in-silicon-valley-michael-seibe @ 02:08](https://youtu.be/oAEZoAp2_zY?t=128)
- He therefore prefers to select companies using the "is this team executing" bar rather than the "is this a good idea" bar [why-fundraising-is-different-in-silicon-valley-michael-seibe @ 02:28](https://youtu.be/oAEZoAp2_zY?t=148)
- Founders discouraged by fundraising failure in their local community shouldn't necessarily conclude they have a bad idea or that they should quit — they should consider fundraising in the Bay Area or another larger startup community instead [why-fundraising-is-different-in-silicon-valley-michael-seibe @ 02:50](https://youtu.be/oAEZoAp2_zY?t=170)
