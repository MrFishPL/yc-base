# Closing Remarks at Startup School NY 2014 — Compiled Truth

Alexis Ohanian closes YC's first-ever Startup School NY by thanking the speakers, volunteer attendees, and crew, confirming non-profits are welcome at YC just like for-profits, and announcing upcoming events.

**Key ideas (with timestamps):**
- Non-profits are just as welcome to apply to YC as for-profit startups, following Watsi as YC's first funded non-profit. [closing-remarks-at-startup-school-ny-2014 @ 00:00](https://youtu.be/ksDTT-pm73k?t=0)
- Attendees were invited to keep giving feedback by tweeting speakers directly or @YCombinator and @AlexisOhanian so YC can keep improving the event format. [closing-remarks-at-startup-school-ny-2014 @ 00:43](https://youtu.be/ksDTT-pm73k?t=43)
- Ohanian thanked the speakers and the randomly-selected attendee startups who presented on stage, noting they had no advance preparation. [closing-remarks-at-startup-school-ny-2014 @ 01:01](https://youtu.be/ksDTT-pm73k?t=61)
- The event's success also depended on the venue crew and YC staff (Tara, Arina, and Kat) working behind the scenes. [closing-remarks-at-startup-school-ny-2014 @ 01:33](https://youtu.be/ksDTT-pm73k?t=93)
- More Startup School events were announced, including one in London the following month and another back in Silicon Valley. [closing-remarks-at-startup-school-ny-2014 @ 01:48](https://youtu.be/ksDTT-pm73k?t=108)
- YC's first-ever Hackathon was announced for August 2nd and 3rd, open for online applications. [closing-remarks-at-startup-school-ny-2014 @ 02:05](https://youtu.be/ksDTT-pm73k?t=125)
