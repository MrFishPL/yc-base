# Elon Musk & The Midwit Meme – Dalton Caldwell and Michael Seibel — Compiled Truth

Overthinking is a founder's biggest mistake: the "midwit meme" shows that naive beginners and true experts often reach the same simple, action-oriented conclusion, while the overthinking middle group talks themselves into paralysis with complex, hedged strategies.

- Founders without constraints or a boss tend to overthink every decision, looping in circles because no one is telling them what to do [elon-musk-the-midwit-meme-dalton-caldwell-and-michael-seibel @ 01:32](https://youtu.be/IYLVhk7yaaw?t=92)
- The worst place to be is knowing just enough to see why something is hard, which causes paralyzing worry — a beginner's mind that doesn't see the obstacles often performs better [elon-musk-the-midwit-meme-dalton-caldwell-and-michael-seibel @ 02:10](https://youtu.be/IYLVhk7yaaw?t=130)
