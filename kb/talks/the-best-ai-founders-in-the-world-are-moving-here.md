# The best AI founders in the world are moving here — Compiled Truth

The YC Lightcone hosts trace San Francisco's arc from post-dot-com "Doom Loop" through the pandemic bust to its post-ChatGPT "Boom Loop," arguing the city's real advantage isn't proximity to capital or talent but the concentrated culture of ambitious builders who normalize working obsessively on hard, uncertain ideas.

**Key ideas (with timestamps):**
- YC itself helped accelerate the founder migration from Palo Alto/Mountain View to San Francisco years earlier than it otherwise would have happened, as young founders clustered in buildings like the "Y-scraper" [the-best-ai-founders-in-the-world-are-moving-here @ 03:59](https://youtu.be/wA0qC9D4LA0?t=239)
- Being physically near other builders you respect measurably increases your own chances of success, which is why proximity to peers who've built things you admire matters [the-best-ai-founders-in-the-world-are-moving-here @ 05:33](https://youtu.be/wA0qC9D4LA0?t=333)
- San Francisco is described as "the place in the world where you can manufacture luck," illustrated by a chance sidewalk meeting with investor Ron Conway that led to a flurry of customer introductions [the-best-ai-founders-in-the-world-are-moving-here @ 09:41](https://youtu.be/wA0qC9D4LA0?t=581)
- The biggest factor drawing founders to Silicon Valley isn't transactional access to investors or hires but a psychological one: people become the average of those they surround themselves with, and ambition is contagious [the-best-ai-founders-in-the-world-are-moving-here @ 14:07](https://youtu.be/wA0qC9D4LA0?t=847)
- Before ChatGPT, working on AI required being a "counterculture misfit" willing to persist through an AI winter when it wasn't a mainstream or prestigious pursuit [the-best-ai-founders-in-the-world-are-moving-here @ 19:18](https://youtu.be/wA0qC9D4LA0?t=1158)
- Remote work during Covid let people who'd tolerated San Francisco's underlying problems (homelessness, crime, cost) finally leave, briefly seeding rival hopes for a distributed "next Silicon Valley" [the-best-ai-founders-in-the-world-are-moving-here @ 18:13](https://youtu.be/wA0qC9D4LA0?t=1093)
- ChatGPT's launch reset the city's trajectory, with essentially all major AI labs (OpenAI, Anthropic, Scale AI) now concentrated in the Bay Area, evidence of strong network effects [the-best-ai-founders-in-the-world-are-moving-here @ 20:31](https://youtu.be/wA0qC9D4LA0?t=1231)
- Post-Covid, which specific neighborhood a founder lives in matters much more than before; YC now advises founders to live within a mile of its Dogpatch office to build lasting relationships through weekly demo nights [the-best-ai-founders-in-the-world-are-moving-here @ 25:23](https://youtu.be/wA0qC9D4LA0?t=1523)
