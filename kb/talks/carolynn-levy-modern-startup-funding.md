# Carolynn Levy - Modern Startup Funding — Compiled Truth

YC's general counsel explains how early-stage startup financing evolved from expensive, slow priced Series A rounds through convertible promissory notes to the SAFE, and why valuation and dilution remain the two things founders must always track.

**Key ideas (with timestamps):**
- The old way to raise a first round was a full Series A preferred stock financing, which took months and could cost $25,000-$100,000 in legal fees [carolynn-levy-modern-startup-funding @ 07:46](https://youtu.be/9qWZALyGSmg?t=466)
- Bridge loans between priced rounds relied on convertible promissory notes, which people eventually realized could be used standalone as a company's very first fundraising instrument [carolynn-levy-modern-startup-funding @ 10:05](https://youtu.be/9qWZALyGSmg?t=605)
- YC created the SAFE (simple agreement for future equity) as a one-document convertible security that, unlike a promissory note, is not debt [carolynn-levy-modern-startup-funding @ 11:30](https://youtu.be/9qWZALyGSmg?t=690)
- Priced rounds are still the modern way startups raise money, they just now tend to happen second rather than first [carolynn-levy-modern-startup-funding @ 13:34](https://youtu.be/9qWZALyGSmg?t=814)
- Because convertible security holders aren't yet stockholders, founders can lose track of how much of their company they've actually sold and get surprised at the priced round when everything converts [carolynn-levy-modern-startup-funding @ 14:51](https://youtu.be/9qWZALyGSmg?t=891)
- Easy, flexible convertible fundraising can lead to "party rounds" with 25-35 different angel investors, which becomes administratively harder to manage once they become stockholders needing consent [carolynn-levy-modern-startup-funding @ 15:55](https://youtu.be/9qWZALyGSmg?t=955)
- Investors who only hold convertible securities (not yet stock) tend to write smaller checks and be less invested day-to-day, which can cut both ways [carolynn-levy-modern-startup-funding @ 16:32](https://youtu.be/9qWZALyGSmg?t=992)
- The only real term to negotiate in a SAFE is valuation, and YC even has a valuation-free version of the SAFE available [carolynn-levy-modern-startup-funding @ 24:53](https://youtu.be/9qWZALyGSmg?t=1493)
- Raising small checks from angels via convertible securities before a priced round is not viewed negatively by VCs — it signals focus and milestone progress [carolynn-levy-modern-startup-funding @ 19:22](https://youtu.be/9qWZALyGSmg?t=1162)
- Communication with investors has always mattered because fundraising is fundamentally about a relationship where investors expect founders to turn their money into a big business [carolynn-levy-modern-startup-funding @ 05:36](https://youtu.be/9qWZALyGSmg?t=336)
