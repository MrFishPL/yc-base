# Why Does Your Company Deserve More Money? by Michael Seibel — Compiled Truth

Michael Seibel argues that founders who burn through their seed round without product-market fit should not assume they deserve more money, and that cutting burn to reach break-even is usually a better path than chasing another raise.

- Founders often cargo-cult the trappings of success — a team, a product, a cool office — but those are means to an end, not evidence the company deserves more capital, just like an NFL coach with zero wins can't point to a great stadium to justify a new contract [why-does-your-company-deserve-more-money-by-michael-seibel @ 00:58](https://youtu.be/xqSyeQ689WM?t=58)
