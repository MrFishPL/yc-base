# The Powerful Alternative To Fine-Tuning — Compiled Truth

Poetic co-founder Ian Fischer describes a recursively self-improving "meta system" that automatically generates reasoning harnesses on top of frontier models, letting startups outperform base LLMs without the sunk cost and obsolescence risk of fine-tuning.
