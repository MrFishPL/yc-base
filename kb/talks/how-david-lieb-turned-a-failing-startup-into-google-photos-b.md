# How David Lieb Turned a Failing Startup Into Google Photos | Backstory — Compiled Truth

David Lieb recounts how his YC startup Bump, a viral hit that flamed out from low engagement, was pivoted twice through direct user conversations into Photo Roll, which became the basis for Google Photos, and how a leukemia diagnosis later reshaped his priorities toward mentoring founders at YC.
