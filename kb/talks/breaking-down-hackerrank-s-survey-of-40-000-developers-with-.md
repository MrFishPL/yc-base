# Breaking Down HackerRank's Survey of 40,000 Developers with Vivek Ravisankar — Compiled Truth

HackerRank co-founder and CEO Vivek Ravisankar traces the company's path through two failed pivots before finding product-market fit matching developers to jobs by skill, and shares findings from their 40,000-developer survey.

**Key ideas (with timestamps):**
- HackerRank's first version, Interview Street, offered paid mock interviews in India but was rejected by YC and failed because students had little cash and scheduling was chaotic [breaking-down-hackerrank-s-survey-of-40-000-developers-with- @ 05:00](https://youtu.be/zYCU5s9Ed0M?t=300)
- Ravisankar reflects that the key lesson he wished he'd internalized earlier was to make a small number of users really love the product rather than chasing every possible company [breaking-down-hackerrank-s-survey-of-40-000-developers-with- @ 05:00](https://youtu.be/zYCU5s9Ed0M?t=300)
- Their second pivot, helping Indian students apply to US master's programs, also failed because demand collapsed to zero outside the twice-yearly application windows [breaking-down-hackerrank-s-survey-of-40-000-developers-with- @ 08:25](https://youtu.be/zYCU5s9Ed0M?t=505)
- The insight that broke the pattern was realizing "if you have to make money, you have to sell something to people who have money," leading them to pivot to selling developer assessment to companies instead of students [breaking-down-hackerrank-s-survey-of-40-000-developers-with- @ 12:10](https://youtu.be/zYCU5s9Ed0M?t=730)
- HackerRank applied to YC three times, finally getting in on the third try after building the company-facing skills platform, and became the first India-based company accepted into YC [breaking-down-hackerrank-s-survey-of-40-000-developers-with- @ 20:29](https://youtu.be/zYCU5s9Ed0M?t=1229)

- The survey's most popular finding was that one in four developers learn to code before they learn to drive, reflecting a broader shift toward self-taught programmers [breaking-down-hackerrank-s-survey-of-40-000-developers-with- @ 27:01](https://youtu.be/zYCU5s9Ed0M?t=1621)
