# Mark Zuckerberg on Taking Risks and Finding Talented People — Compiled Truth

Mark Zuckerberg reflects on Facebook's early formation, Peter Thiel's advice on risk-taking, and how Facebook hires and promotes raw talent over prior experience.

**Key ideas (with timestamps):**
- Facebook only became a formal Delaware company about six months in, when Peter Thiel invested, even though Zuckerberg and Dustin Moskovitz initially told him they planned to go back to school [mark-zuckerberg-on-taking-risks-and-finding-talented-people @ 00:00](https://youtu.be/VAUt2j6juHU?t=0).
- They kept telling Harvard they were taking "one more semester off" until eventually deciding not to go back at all [mark-zuckerberg-on-taking-risks-and-finding-talented-people @ 00:49](https://youtu.be/VAUt2j6juHU?t=49).
- Peter Thiel's best piece of advice was that in a world changing quickly, the biggest risk you can take is not taking any risk [mark-zuckerberg-on-taking-risks-and-finding-talented-people @ 01:04](https://youtu.be/VAUt2j6juHU?t=64).
- People tend to focus on the downside risk of any single decision, but staying stagnant and never making big shifts guarantees failure over time [mark-zuckerberg-on-taking-risks-and-finding-talented-people @ 01:45](https://youtu.be/VAUt2j6juHU?t=105).
- Since Zuckerberg started the company at 19, Facebook can't institutionally believe prior experience is essential, so it invests in people it judges as raw talent even without a track record in that specific role [mark-zuckerberg-on-taking-risks-and-finding-talented-people @ 02:23](https://youtu.be/VAUt2j6juHU?t=143).
- You can assess someone's raw talent from side projects and other things they've done, without requiring they've already done the exact job [mark-zuckerberg-on-taking-risks-and-finding-talented-people @ 02:59](https://youtu.be/VAUt2j6juHU?t=179).
- Facebook has run about 12 product groups, and all but one leader grew into that role internally rather than joining already running it, which Zuckerberg calls a point of pride [mark-zuckerberg-on-taking-risks-and-finding-talented-people @ 03:49](https://youtu.be/VAUt2j6juHU?t=229).
- Giving people at the company real opportunity to grow is what keeps the best people engaged and attracts more top talent who see a path to those opportunities [mark-zuckerberg-on-taking-risks-and-finding-talented-people @ 04:24](https://youtu.be/VAUt2j6juHU?t=264).
