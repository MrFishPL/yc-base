# Lessons From The Founders Scaling Their Startup In A War Zone — Compiled Truth

Stacy and Roman, founders of a Ukraine-based design-matching startup, describe evacuating their team, coping with the trauma of Russia's invasion, and continuing to operate and grow the business during the war.

- The founders ran a platform matching companies with designers for 24-hour turnaround design tasks, with over 100 designers connected, mostly based in Ukraine [lessons-from-the-founders-scaling-their-startup-in-a-war-zon @ 00:28](https://youtu.be/y7Zz3TmXNHY?t=28)
