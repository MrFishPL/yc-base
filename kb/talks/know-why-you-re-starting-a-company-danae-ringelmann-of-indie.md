# Know Why You're Starting a Company - Danae Ringelmann of Indiegogo — Compiled Truth

Danae Ringelmann argues that a founder's authentic "why" is the single most practical asset in a company, driving co-founder fit, pivots, strategy, hiring, and customer loyalty.

**Key ideas (with timestamps):**
- If a founder's reason for starting the company isn't authentic to their core, the chances of failing go way up [know-why-you-re-starting-a-company-danae-ringelmann-of-indie @ 00:00](https://youtu.be/Q4GJKcP_2FQ?t=0)
- Ringelmann's why, to democratize access to capital, is what found her co-founders, since spewing that why to the world attracted people who cared about the same problem [know-why-you-re-starting-a-company-danae-ringelmann-of-indie @ 00:36](https://youtu.be/Q4GJKcP_2FQ?t=36)
- Being more obsessed with the why than with the original solution let her pivot from an offline fund idea to an online perks-based crowdfunding model that became Indiegogo [know-why-you-re-starting-a-company-danae-ringelmann-of-indie @ 01:08](https://youtu.be/Q4GJKcP_2FQ?t=68)
- A strong why gets founders through dark periods, such as when Indiegogo's first funding round took three and a half years instead of the planned few months [know-why-you-re-starting-a-company-danae-ringelmann-of-indie @ 01:56](https://youtu.be/Q4GJKcP_2FQ?t=116)
- The why informs company strategy directly, for example Indiegogo stayed an open, gatekeeper-free platform and went global from the start because the mission was to empower everybody in the world [know-why-you-re-starting-a-company-danae-ringelmann-of-indie @ 03:09](https://youtu.be/Q4GJKcP_2FQ?t=189)
- A clear mission attracts amazing talent, since top people who are already comfortable will change situations for a company that lets them do what they believe is right [know-why-you-re-starting-a-company-danae-ringelmann-of-indie @ 04:53](https://youtu.be/Q4GJKcP_2FQ?t=293)
- A strong why also attracts customers, illustrated by engineers who raised over $2 million for solar-powered roads and a genome-sequencing team that raised $350,000 on Indiegogo before getting into YC and closing a $4.5 million seed round [know-why-you-re-starting-a-company-danae-ringelmann-of-indie @ 05:36](https://youtu.be/Q4GJKcP_2FQ?t=336)
- Founders can find clarity on their why using the "5 whys" exercise: state why you're starting the company, then keep asking why again until you reach an irrational, belief-level answer [know-why-you-re-starting-a-company-danae-ringelmann-of-indie @ 06:38](https://youtu.be/Q4GJKcP_2FQ?t=398)
