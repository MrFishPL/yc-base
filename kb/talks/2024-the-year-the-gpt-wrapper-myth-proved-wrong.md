# 2024: The Year the GPT Wrapper Myth Proved Wrong — Compiled Truth

The YC Lightcone hosts review 2024 and argue the "GPT wrapper" and "foundation-model-captures-all-value" narratives were both proven wrong, as startups built durable, fast-growing businesses on top of LLMs while pilots converted into real enterprise revenue.

**Key ideas (with timestamps):**
- Startups can now reach tens of millions in revenue within 24 months while spending only $2-5M, a pace and capital efficiency that felt implausible a year earlier [2024-the-year-the-gpt-wrapper-myth-proved-wrong @ 00:00](https://youtu.be/z0wt2pe_LZM?t=0)
- The "ChatGPT will crush every app built on top of it" consensus (crystallized around the GPT Store launch) proved wrong; breakout apps like Perplexity (consumer) and Glean (enterprise), plus verticals like legal tech (Harvey), were built by outside startups, not OpenAI [2024-the-year-the-gpt-wrapper-myth-proved-wrong @ 01:11](https://youtu.be/z0wt2pe_LZM?t=71)
- The parallel fear that only foundation-model companies (or those with huge capital) could capture value also proved false, driven partly by Meta's Llama release becoming a top-ranked open model and giving startups real model choice [2024-the-year-the-gpt-wrapper-myth-proved-wrong @ 02:38](https://youtu.be/z0wt2pe_LZM?t=158)
- With model choice available, competitive advantage shifted to product, sales ability, responsiveness to user feedback, and retention rather than to which model a company used [2024-the-year-the-gpt-wrapper-myth-proved-wrong @ 04:23](https://youtu.be/z0wt2pe_LZM?t=263)
- What started as "model routers" to cut cost evolved into full multi-model orchestration architectures (fast/cheap models for simple tasks, larger models for complex ones), seen across Fall 2024 batch companies like Cambio (PDF parsing) and fraud-detection startups using a "junior risk analyst" pattern [2024-the-year-the-gpt-wrapper-myth-proved-wrong @ 06:36](https://youtu.be/z0wt2pe_LZM?t=396)
- Enterprise pilots that were dismissed a year earlier as likely to stay proofs-of-concept converted into real, large-scale revenue and deployments, with YC companies now hitting milestones like $1M ARR faster than ever observed [2024-the-year-the-gpt-wrapper-myth-proved-wrong @ 09:08](https://youtu.be/z0wt2pe_LZM?t=548)
- Summer and Fall 2024 YC batches grew in aggregate at roughly the historic "top quartile only" pace of 10%/week (3x growth over the batch), a rate the hosts say had never happened in aggregate before [2024-the-year-the-gpt-wrapper-myth-proved-wrong @ 10:06](https://youtu.be/z0wt2pe_LZM?t=606)
- Founders who couldn't find an idea during their batch are pivoting into AI ideas afterward and succeeding, illustrated by a founder who built an AI back-office for dentist offices after his batch ended and is now seeing strong week-over-week growth [2024-the-year-the-gpt-wrapper-myth-proved-wrong @ 19:14](https://youtu.be/z0wt2pe_LZM?t=1154)
