# Helping African Businesses Get Paid, Shola Akinlade of Paystack — Compiled Truth

Shola Akinlade, co-founder of Paystack, describes building a payments company in Nigeria from a one-year underground beta with a waitlist through YC and into a fast-growing pan-African fintech, emphasizing team quality, customer obsession, and patience with local infrastructure constraints.

**Key ideas (with timestamps):**
- Before applying to YC, Akinlade spent about a year quietly building Paystack, talking to a waitlist of roughly 300 customers to understand what they wanted [helping-african-businesses-get-paid-shola-akinlade-of-paysta @ 02:09](https://youtu.be/3_t7kHENrxg?t=129)
- His YC application and interview were kept extremely simple and concrete (e.g., "we've processed $200"), and he believes YC's process is optimized to find the best founders regardless of geography [helping-african-businesses-get-paid-shola-akinlade-of-paysta @ 04:22](https://youtu.be/3_t7kHENrxg?t=262)
- A YC partner told him to remove the artificial waitlist barrier to growth rather than gate access over fraud worries [helping-african-businesses-get-paid-shola-akinlade-of-paysta @ 09:14](https://youtu.be/3_t7kHENrxg?t=554)
- He learned that the way to build a strong network is to build something impressive, rather than networking directly [helping-african-businesses-get-paid-shola-akinlade-of-paysta @ 11:33](https://youtu.be/3_t7kHENrxg?t=693)
- A mentor told him it takes about seven years to build a solid startup, which reframed his sense of urgency and patience [helping-african-businesses-get-paid-shola-akinlade-of-paysta @ 12:17](https://youtu.be/3_t7kHENrxg?t=737)
- Paystack's early growth strategy centered on making transactions succeed reliably and building an automated dispute process, since a bad payment experience could permanently scare a user off digital payments [helping-african-businesses-get-paid-shola-akinlade-of-paysta @ 15:41](https://youtu.be/3_t7kHENrxg?t=941)

- Akinlade's biggest lesson was learning to find and hire people better than himself, moving out of doing customer success, design, and support himself within a month of joining YC [helping-african-businesses-get-paid-shola-akinlade-of-paysta @ 35:10](https://youtu.be/3_t7kHENrxg?t=2110)
