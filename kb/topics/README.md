# kb/topics/

**What goes here:** cross-cutting ideas / frameworks / recurring questions — `<kebab>.md`,
`type: concept`. Two-layer page: Compiled Truth (current YC view) above `---`, dated + cited
Timeline below. This is the **canonical home** for repeated advice — "talk to users", "default
alive", etc. live here once and are cited by many talk pages.

**What does NOT go here:** single-talk summaries (→ `kb/talks/`) or people/companies
(→ `kb/entities/`). If two topics both seem to fit a fact, pick the most specific and backlink.
