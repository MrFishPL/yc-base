# Consumer Startups — Compiled Truth

YC's collective advice on consumer: nail the product and a real "magic moment" of retention before touching growth tactics, since organic word-of-mouth (not paid acquisition or clever marketing) is what separates breakout consumer companies from the rest; AI is now reopening consumer categories long thought dead (previously too expensive to personalize, too capital-intensive to build, or killed by lack of trust/data) even though investor appetite for consumer swings in and out of fashion and distribution remains the hardest unsolved problem.
