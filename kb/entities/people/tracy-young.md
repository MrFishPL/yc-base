# Tracy Young — Compiled Truth

Co-founder and former CEO of PlanGrid, a construction-blueprint software company she built with
non-technical domain expertise and grit into a 450-person company acquired by Autodesk. Across her
talks she returns to a few consistent themes: obsessive user focus born from her own field
experience, "do things that don't scale" as the only real path through early hypergrowth, the
discipline of hiring/firing fast, and the emotional toll of founding — including how her family's
story as Vietnam War refugees shaped the resilience that carried her through it.
