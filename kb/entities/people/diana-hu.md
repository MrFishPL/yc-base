# Diana Hu — Compiled Truth

Diana Hu is a YC Group Partner and technical founder — she co-founded and was CTO of Escher Reality (6D.ai), an AR/SLAM startup acquired by Niantic, and named her earlier company Scripted. Across her talks she recurs to a few consistent positions: technical founders should personally build the full stack rather than delegate early [[building-product]]; startups should ruthlessly favor iteration speed and "do things that don't scale" over engineering polish before product-market fit [[mvp]], [[do-things-that-dont-scale]]; and, more recently, that AI should be architected as a company's core operating system — a self-monitoring "closed loop" — rather than a bolt-on productivity tool [[ai-strategy]]. She also speaks candidly about co-founder conflict and immigrant-founder psychology from her own experience [[founder-psychology]], [[co-founders]].
