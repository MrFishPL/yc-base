# Andy Bromberg — Compiled Truth

CoinList co-founder/CEO who previously built and wound down Sidewire, an expert-political-discussion platform, and who teaches at YC's Startup Investor School alongside Aaron Harris. Across his talks he threads two consistent positions: (1) a historical view of early-stage investing as a 70-80 year march toward lower cost to start a company, lower cost/barrier to invest, and faster liquidity — from 1940s friends-and-family checks through YC's convertible notes and SAFE to the 2018 ICO boom [andy-bromberg-startup-investor-school-day-4 @ 06:43](https://youtu.be/T6g2D92H1FQ?t=403); and (2) skepticism that tokens/ICOs are a good fit for most startups — they only make sense where parties don't trust each other and don't want a central intermediary either, and CoinList rejects the vast majority of the thousands of token-sale pitches it receives [a-conversation-about-crypto-currencies-and-icos-with-andy-br @ 40:04](https://youtu.be/q_NbKjVc2l8?t=2404). He also gives practical angel-investing advice: be helpful and honest to build the deal flow that comes from founder word-of-mouth, avoid heavy terms, and get out of the way after closing [startup-investor-school-day-4-live-stream @ 1:24:49](https://youtu.be/CKBeSyquRec?t=5089).
