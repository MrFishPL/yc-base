# Ben Silbermann — Compiled Truth

Co-founder and former CEO of Pinterest, who pivoted from a failed iPhone catalog app (Tote) into
a niche visual product that took years of scrappy, in-person community-building before it found
its people. Across his talks he returns to a few recurring positions: build something you and
your co-founder genuinely want for yourselves rather than from market research; treat early
distribution as a community problem to be solved person-by-person, not an engineering problem to
be automated; be relentlessly scrappy and personally involved in support, hiring, and watching
users struggle; and hold that a founder's unchanging job — no matter how large the company gets —
is driving clarity on what's being built, why, and what the culture rewards.
