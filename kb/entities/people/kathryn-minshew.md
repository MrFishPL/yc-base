# Kathryn Minshew — Compiled Truth

Kathryn Minshew is the founder of The Muse (YC-backed), a career platform born out of frustration with the job-search process. Across her talks she returns to a consistent set of themes: ship an ugly, imperfect MVP and let real strangers (not friends) validate it; grow scrappily through manual, personal outreach and content when there's no marketing budget; treat culture and shared values as strategic assets rather than nice-to-haves; and reassure other founders that early-stage flat growth, rejection, and death-defying risk are universal and do ease with time.
