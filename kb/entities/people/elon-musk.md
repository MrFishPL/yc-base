# Elon Musk — Compiled Truth

Founder of Zip2, X.com/PayPal, SpaceX, Tesla, and xAI, and a recurring YC/Startup School speaker. Across his talks he pushes a consistent decision framework — optimize for "usefulness" (utility delta times number of people affected) rather than glory or risk-adjusted return — and repeatedly cites SpaceX and Tesla as bets he expected would probably fail (sub-10% odds) but pursued anyway because the alternative was stagnation. He argues fear is normal and should be overridden by the importance of the goal, that technology only improves through deliberate effort and can regress, and that AI, genetics, and brain-computer bandwidth are the highest-leverage problems for humanity's future. He also emphasizes that the large majority of his own time (~80%) goes to hands-on engineering and design rather than business or media work.
