# Justin Kan — Compiled Truth

Justin.tv/Twitch co-founder (and Exec, Atrium founder) who is YC's go-to voice on whether to join a startup as an employee, and who also speaks on hands-on early growth tactics and press. His recurring throughline is that a startup job is a bet on personal learning speed and access to outsized responsibility rather than money or stability, and that early traction — whether users or press — comes from scrappy, deliberate, unscalable effort rather than shortcuts.
