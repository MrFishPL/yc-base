# Tom Blomfield — Compiled Truth

YC Group Partner and co-founder of two billion-dollar fintech startups, GoCardless and Monzo, who
now teaches B2B pricing and metrics discipline alongside a fast-growing focus on AI coding agents.
His recurring throughline across founder-story, metrics, and AI talks is that discipline compounds:
launch early and do things that don't scale, track a small set of consistently-defined metrics
(especially revenue and net dollar retention) instead of vanity numbers, price on quantified value
rather than cost, and — in the AI era — use tight, git-disciplined workflows with coding agents to
build software radically faster while still anchoring on real human problems that are hard to find
even as building becomes easy.
