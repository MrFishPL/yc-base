# Kevin Hale — Compiled Truth

Cofounder of **Wufoo** (bootstrapped a 100,000-subscriber blog audience before writing code, sold to SurveyMonkey) and now a Y Combinator Group Partner who teaches Startup School's core lecture series on pricing, conversion, pitching, idea evaluation, and co-founder relationships, and also interviews founders/operators (HubSpot's Brian Halligan, Zapier's Mike Knoop, Second Measure's Michael Babineau) about growth and go-to-market. His recurring throughline is that founders should replace fear and intuition with first-principles frameworks — for pricing, for clarity of language, for diagnosing conversion, and for evaluating both startup ideas and co-founder conflict — and he draws heavily on his own Wufoo experience (treating early users like a relationship that decays without constant reinvestment) as a worked example throughout.
