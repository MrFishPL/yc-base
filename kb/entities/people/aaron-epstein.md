# Aaron Epstein — Compiled Truth

YC Group Partner and ex-Creative Market founder who teaches funnel-driven, highly quantitative
approaches to early growth — cold outreach, pricing, and business-model selection — and also runs
hands-on design critiques of founder-submitted mobile apps and websites alongside outside
operators (Glide's David Siegel, Webflow's Vlad Magdalin). His recurring throughline is that early
traction is a numbers-and-mechanics problem: map the funnel backwards, test real users' reactions
directly, and price and design deliberately rather than by default or fear.
