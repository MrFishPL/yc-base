# Elizabeth Iorns — Compiled Truth

Elizabeth Iorns is the founder of Science Exchange, a two-sided B2B marketplace connecting researchers with outsourced experimental services, and serves as a YC expert-partner for biotech companies. Across her talks she returns to a consistent set of positions: biotech founders don't need a separate business co-founder because scientific training already builds transferable skills [elizabeth-iorns-on-biotech-companies-in-yc @ 06:43](https://youtu.be/PzkyHKHRH2w?t=403); YC's three-month batch is used to de-risk technology and nail go-to-market rather than to make fundamental scientific breakthroughs [elizabeth-iorns-on-biotech-companies-in-yc @ 03:17](https://youtu.be/PzkyHKHRH2w?t=197); and founders should solve a problem that has personally affected them, as she did when she founded Science Exchange out of her own frustration as a breast cancer researcher [elizabeth-iorns-at-female-founders-conference-2014 @ 02:28](https://youtu.be/CII2SmXOB60?t=148).
