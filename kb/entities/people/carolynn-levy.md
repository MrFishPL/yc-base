# Carolynn Levy — Compiled Truth

Carolynn Levy is YC's General Counsel and the creator of the SAFE (simple agreement for future equity). Across her talks she consistently pushes founders and investors toward simple, standardized legal mechanics: use the SAFE instead of debt-like convertible notes, negotiate as few terms as possible, incorporate properly and early, vest founder stock, and read/understand every legal document before signing it.
