# Ooshma Garg — Compiled Truth

Founder and CEO of Gobble, who tells and retells the multi-year story of nearly killing the
company several times — a failed peer-to-peer chef marketplace, a failed on-demand delivery pivot
("Gobble Instant"), an enterprise-catering detour that scared off investors, and two rejected
Series A rounds — before finding product-market fit with a personalized, 10-15 minute dinner-kit
subscription. Her recurring themes: a shared mission (not founder ambition alone) is what carries
a team through the "trough of sorrow"; iterating (carrying forward learnings) beats pivoting
(starting over); staying close to the customer is both the diagnostic tool and the emotional
anchor for a CEO; and product-market fit is a cumulative summation of small experiments, defined
concretely as the intersection of growth and retention.
