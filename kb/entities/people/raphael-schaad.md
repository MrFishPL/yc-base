# Raphael Schaad — Compiled Truth

Designer-founder who built Cron (acquired by Notion, where he built Notion Calendar), and now
appears as a recurring YC guest design reviewer. His throughline across talks is that design is a
core founder skill, not decoration — spanning desirability, viability, and feasibility — and that
AI is shifting interface design from static "nouns" (buttons, forms) to dynamic "verbs"
(workflows, autocomplete, autonomous agent actions), which demands new interaction patterns,
prototyping approaches, and a founder's active taste to avoid generic AI-default output.
