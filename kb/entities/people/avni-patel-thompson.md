# Avni Patel Thompson — Compiled Truth

Founder of childcare marketplace Poppy (and, before that, a failed cultural-subscription box) and
a YC alum who speaks widely about the founder journey from scrappy validation to eventual shutdown.
Her recurring throughline is that starting is the only real test of an idea: validate cheaply and
fast with real users and real money before building anything, then treat consistent week-over-week
growth as the compass for both raising money and finding co-founders — and, when the economics
never work, wind down transparently and refuse to treat that as failure.
