# Diane Greene — Compiled Truth

VMware co-founder and later CEO of Google Cloud (following Google's acquisition of her startup
Bebop), Diane Greene draws on founding three companies — VExtreme, VMware, and Bebop — to argue
that founder alignment, culture, and tenacity matter more than any single tactic. She repeatedly
stresses picking board members and early investors for technical understanding and specific
expertise gaps rather than money or prestige, breaking into enterprise markets through hardware
partnerships when a direct sales force isn't feasible, and framing a disruptive innovation so it's
easy for incumbents to adopt.
