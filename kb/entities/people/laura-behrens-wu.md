# Laura Behrens Wu — Compiled Truth

Laura Behrens Wu is the co-founder of Shippo, a shipping-API startup that abstracts a fragmented, non-technical carrier industry into a single dashboard for ecommerce merchants. Across her talks she returns to a consistent playbook: build painkillers rather than vitamins, be relentlessly persistent through investor rejection while staying flexible on tactics, focus the whole company on one KPI, and treat investors and culture-building with the same rigor founders apply to hiring.
